Busy Bodies

Hi ! I'm Eileen of the Process Review and Improvement Office. I just would like to give you an account of what is  happening with the busy buddies.

Have you seen us lately?  Well, I know that summer was supposed to have made us look slimmer due to our workload and yet -- hold your hands high -- we're still the fit people you all used to see.

The last term of the school year 1998-1999 has been fruitful for us in PRIO. For one, the Disbursement Process Review handled by Ms. Marivic and Ella is approximately 90% completed.

Simultaneously, the Internal Quality Audit Report  by Amy for the above process has been presented to Ms. Gloria de Leon and Ms. Cynthia Lim. But they requested for a re-evaluation of the report.

On the other hand, the Pre-enrollment Process Review of Amy and yours truly is now on the streamlining phase meaning a series of PRIT  meetings almost every week --- PHEW!

The CFO will also be undergoing IQA for this term. So CFO people, better watch out for the IQ auditors.

In spite all these tasks, Amy and Ella still managed to attend and complete the Structured Systems Analysis Design training.

Our office is also being renovated so it means new cubicle assignments (Yehey !) --- I'm dying to know where I will be seated.

Well, that is all for now. I'll update you again next issue.

Eileen D. Manabat 06/30/1999
