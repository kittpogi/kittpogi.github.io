    SMO Updates
The FAM Project

The Faculty Attendance Monitoring (FAM) System is currently being developed by Catalina M. Quiogue, Stephanie G. Gonzaga, Lichelle B. Legutan, and Lorna P. Acorda. The system is due for completion this 1st term.

The EVA Project

The EVAluation (EVA) System that was designed by Harold D. Bautista, as assisted by Arfeli B. Balanza, was the second system to implement prototyping as part of the system design process. The system is currently being developed 5 SITE students – Irvin, Don, Ryan, Noy and Grace. The system is also due for completion this 1st term.

The FIS Project

Up until last term, faculty contracts were printed using the old Plantilla Monitoring System (PMS). This 1st Term SY 1999-2000 will be the first term contract printing will be run from the Faculty Information System (FIS). The inclusion of the contract printing as part of the new FIS was spearheaded by Rowena A. Santos.

Y2K Work

The project has been divided into 5 main steps: (1) inventory of all programs; (2) identification of programs that are not Y2K-compliant; (3) revision of non-compliant micro-based programs/conversion of VAX Cobol/Rdb Student Records Management programs to Oracle/Pro-Cobol; (4) testing of revised/converted programs; and (5) implementation of the programs.

The inventory of all programs is currently being done by Rowena A. Santos, Harold D. Bautista, Catalina M. Quiogue, Rhaniel C. Bingcang, Loveleah V. Malig, Lorna P. Acorda, Brian O. Co, and Vivian DC. Sy. Target completion of steps 2 until 5 for micro-based programs is end of August; for VAX Cobol/Rdb programs, end of September.

Vivian DC. Sy 06/30/1999

ISTC Attendance Monitoring (IAM)

The new attendance monitoring system of ISTC is now being used. The system was initially designed to replace the old CSC Attendance Monitoring (CAM) System. With the new IAM, hard copies of the monthly attendance report will no longer be produced since ISTPs can now query about their Daily Time Records online. Coordinators can query on the DTR, Summary of Attendance and Residency Schedule of the staff belonging to their unit.

For compliance to the new implementation guidelines, IAM is undergoing some revisions. One of the revisions is regarding the new log-in/log-out policy concerning tardiness. If an ISTP is more than an hour late, he/she will be required to avail of a forced leave, i.e. vacation leaves (VL) for those entitled to VL, and leave without pay (LWOP) for the rest. Please refer to the ISTP Implementation Guidelines for other details.

Lichelle B. Legutan 07/05/1999

Computer Laboratory Office Information System (CLOIS)

CLOIS, or the Computer Laboratory Office Information System is nearing completion. Ampel is just about finished in designing the program specifications for the system. CLOIS has been designed to handle the scheduling and monitoring of the computer laboratories in campus and the management of Student Assistants who keep watch over these labs.

Brian O. Co 07/02/1999
