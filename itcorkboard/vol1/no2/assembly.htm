ISTP Elects Assembly Officers

Last June 16, 1999, the nominated ISTP assembly officers elected among themselves the officers for the positions identified. In the closed-door event, each one was given a paper where he/she will write his/her chosen nominee for each position. The results are as follows:

President: James Sy
Vice-President: Marivic Tangkeko
Treasurer: Nick Bonao
Secretary: Celine Rivera
ISTC Representatives: Vivian Sy; Jaycee Ibay
Grievance Comm Rep: Ampel Balanza

The function of the ISTP Assembly is to monitor the implementation of the PSI Faculty Manual and the ISTP Implementing Guidelines. It shall initiate the organization of ISTP activities, handle grievance/complaints and look into other benefits for ISTP. One concern that needs looking into is if there is a need to formalize the organization of the assembly. The Vice-President was designated to research on this.

Celine V. Rivera 06/30/1999
