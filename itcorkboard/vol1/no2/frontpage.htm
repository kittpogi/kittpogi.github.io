DLS-PSI Faculty Manual

The Board of Trustees of the DLS-PSI last April 16, 1999 has already signed the DLSU Professional Schools Inc. Faculty Manual for the School Years 1999 – 2002. Members of the Technical Panel were: Dean Lydia B. Echauz and Dr. Pablo Mangaran from GSB; Dean Jonathan Dayao, Philip Chan, and Kai Shan Fernandez from CCS; and our very own Sammy Mallare and Nick Bonao. The Technical Panel was headed by the DLS-PSI Executive Vice-President, Mr. Glenn Sipin.

Among the major additions from the last year’s manual is the sensational inclusion of the rice and laundry subsidy which was later amended for cancellation by a majority vote among the ISTPs. Another is the option to teach within DLSU-Manila (outside residency hours). And the most preferred addition was the conversion of unused sick leaves (maximum of 10) for the school year to its cash equivalent.

The faculty manual is stipulated for us to know clearly our rights and benefits and serves as our guide on how to properly assert and avail these privileges. We should also make the stipulated concessions as motivating factors for each of us to satisfactorily carry out our duties and responsibilities.

DLSU Gets ORACLE Financials

With consultation and coordination between System Finance, Accounting, Internal Auditor, and IT offices, De La Salle University System selected Oracle Financials/Payroll as its Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. This will take care of automating various operations in all five campuses. This will also solve all Y2K-related concerns for some computerized systems.
