    CFO Updates

Internet Account Automation

With the help of DAO (c/o CSCJCS), we were able to automate our internet account activation process starting 01 June 1999.

Anti-Virus Supplier

After several months of evaluation, we’ve narrowed down our choices from five (5) suppliers to one (1) supplier of the Anti-Virus program. The program is cheap, effective and easy to use. We’ll do more tests to make sure that it really works.

Pegasus Mail Upgrade

We’re in the process of upgrading all Pegasus Mail for DOS installation to Pegasus for Windows.

PC Donation

We’re donating some of our PC386 and PC486 to all La Salle supervised schools. Last May, we gave five (5) units of PC386SX/DX with hard disk and five (5) units of PC486DX to La Salle Victorias, five (5) units of PC486DX to St. Michael Parochial School in Jala-Jala Rizal c/o Bro. Jun Erguiza, and five (5) units of PC486DX for another La Salle supervised school c/o Rico Cordero.

Mail Utility Migration

We are migrating from using sendmail utility program to postfix mail utility program. The purpose in doing this is for efficiency and security.

New Members

Rachel C. Zuleta, our Jr. Help Desk, resigned last 15 May 1999 and she was replaced by Mary Jean B. Soria who reported last 16 April of 1999. We also welcome back Mr. Noel C. Castillo as PC/Network Manager starting 16 March 1999.

Nick M. Bonao 07/02/1999
