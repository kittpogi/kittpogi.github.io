New Face for the New School Year

Our Web Development Team has again come up with a new look for the DLSU-Manila web site. Unlike the pastel green theme of the previous web page, the new look is an elegant combination of green and white which is basically Lasallian. The first noticeable item in the web site is the animated graphic messages welcoming the freshmen and advertising the University as an institution where the foundation of one’s future ought to be laid. The next eye-catching things are the sepia rendered icons at the top which, to the delight of most users, becomes colored upon hovering over of the mouse pointer and displays a short description of the page following the link. Also, the façade of the La Salle Building on the left side looks very serene.

Another change is that, the web page now maximizes the whole width of the browser making it appear to have a full-packed information to offer, which speaks true of our web site. We can say straightforward that the DLSU web site is more than just beautifully made custom graphics, Java scripts, etc. It is a parade of useful information both for the Lasallians and especially for the non-Lasallians. The Web Development Team sees to it that every visit to the DLSU home is worth the time (and wait).

One major glitch is, several links are under construction. This is due to the fact that the site is still in its metamorphosis stage and it is such a big site to just change overnight. The layout of every page should be well adjusted to the theme without making it too monotonous for the readers. And every page should be a distinctively Lasallian page. Despite that, it is an honor to say that our site is again nominated to The 2nd Philippine Webby Awards - Children/Education category. The Webby Awards is like the Famas or Oscars of the Internet. So what are you staring at? Visit the 1999 Philippine Webby Awards site to cast your vote!

Joie B. Alvarez 07/02/1999
