Web Page Updates

All the information for the school year were posted on the web. This include names of new administrators, school calendar (academic/non-academic), new fees table, and other updates by the office units.

Redesign of the web page
With the new design of the DLSU web main page (see related story), we are presently converting our internal pages to be consistent with the new layout.

DLSU Intranet

We are preparing for the redesign and reorganization of the DLSU Intranet before its first year anniversary on August this year. We have distributed survey questionnaires thru the email and thru internal mail (for those without email accounts) to get a feedback on the effectiveness and efficiency of the present design of the Intranet before we do the works. Target respondents are representatives of administrative personnel, teaching faculty, co-academic personnel (formerly the non academic staff), and students.

Among the new information that we plan to add are course syllabi, faculty lecture notes, degree flowcharts, faculty/student directory of email addresses, course schedules by course code/faculty, department/unit functions and services, price list of office and computer supplies, University strategic plans, job description, employee personal information and a lot more.

Among the additional features that we wish to include are sending of employee/student information updates thru the web; query on service/academic record, loans, leave balances; discussion group, frequently asked questions; download section; submission of forms (reservation, request for services/repairs); online registration to University events and many more.

Of course, all these need coordination with the other units of ISTC and administrative units concerned.

Celine V. Rivera 07/02/1999

Adobe® Software Demonstrations

For the past month, the Documentation Office staff has attended demonstrations on Adobe PhotoShop 5.0, Adobe ImageReady 1.0 and Adobe Acrobat 4.0.

Adobe PhotoShop 5.0

Exciting new features are added in this latest version of Adobe PhotoShop. Among the standouts are the Multiple Undo and Redo levels in the History palette, Layer Effects, Automated plug-ins, and new tools such as the Magnet tool and the Editable text tool.

Adobe ImageReady 1.0

Optimizing images for the Web is a constant trade-off between quality and file size. Adobe ImageReady helps in preparing images for the World Wide Web more efficiently. It can optimize, compress and animate graphics created in Adobe PhotoShop, Adobe Illustrator, and other graphics packages.

Adobe Acrobat 4.0

Adobe Acrobat software can convert documents to universally accessible Adobe Portable Document Format (PDF) files and then distribute them electronically across platforms and applications while preserving their original look – complete with fonts, graphics and layout.

Francis Mark Santos 07/02/1999
