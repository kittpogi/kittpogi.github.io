Employment Information System

What are the System Description, Objectives, Scope?

Currently, the OCS has 5 major services. These are:

  • Graduate Resume Service
  • Job Opening Posting
  • Graduate Listing and Subscription
  • Graduate Employment Information System
  • ORIENT3

As of this moment, all these services offered by the OCS are done manually. Although they get their work done effectively, the need for them to make their work efficient has arisen. And this is where ISTC-SMO comes into the picture. As part of the ISTC-SMO, the SITE students were tasked to come up with a proposal that will help the OCS achieve the efficiency desired in their work. Currently the proponents of the project have proposed in general that majority of the work would be automated. Different papers that are currently being used would make way for a database that will store their data.

Who are the Project/Target Users?

Just as the services of the OCS is divided into 5 parts, so will this project be. The project would be divided into 5 basic modules that will eventually be integrated. The target users are the employees and student representatives of the OCS. As for the beneficiaries of this project, it would be the companies and the students of DLSU.

When is the start date of project and estimated completion date?

Start date of the project was May 1999 and the projected completion date would be late December 1999. The phases included in the whole project range from systems investigation, analysis, then design, and lastly implementation.

What is the group assigned to do?

The group has been tasked and guided by the ISTC-SMO to do every phase of this project because the SITE students want this project to be their proposal for their ETP proposal.

What is the current working status?

As of the moment, the proponents are in the design phase, specifically the design of the Entity Relationship Diagram of the database that would be used.
