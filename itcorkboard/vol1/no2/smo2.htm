SITE Profile

We have new cool and talented dudes and babes working with us at ISTC until August 1999. They are computer enthusiasts who help us with some of our projects at hand in System Management Office (SMO). The team is composed of two groups of four and five: one, working with EVA (Evaluation System) and the other, EIS (Employment Information System) for OCS (Office of Career Services). They started going to office last April 1999. If you want to meet them personally, they are currently stationed close to our mainland ISTC, i.e. at G306 doing serious business and, well, sometimes not so serious matters.

Here’s some juicy stuff that you may want to know about each of the SITE members. Each practicumee already has a homepage. You can visit their sites from ISTC-SMO’s web page.
