    ISTP Service Awardees

Featuring... the ISTP Service Awardees

This year, four ISTPs will be awarded Service Award for 10 years of service to the University. The awardees are Sammy Mallare (ISTC Director), Celine Rivera (DAO & DOCU Coordinator), Nick Bonao (CFO Coordinator) and Vivian Sy (SMO Coordinator). It cannot be denied that all of them have contributed a lot for the enhancement of the various services of the Center. These pioneers are also among the core reasons why the ISTC was able to expand the services rendered to the DLSU Community and even reaching out to the System Schools. They have piously committed their skills and talents to fully utilize the IT resources of the University for its full advantage. The awardees are expected to serve as role models for each and every one of us and may the dedication to service they have personified through those years be cultivated in us as well.

Let us show them our appreciation by attending the awarding ceremony on July 21, 1999, 12:45pm at the William Shaw Theatre. Nothing to worry, for those having voracious appetite, food will be served after the program. Well, looks like a double treat for us!

Joie B. Alvarez 06/30/1999
