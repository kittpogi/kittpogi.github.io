Pre-paid Printing Cards Unleashed

After several months of preparation and planning, the first ever Prepaid Printing Card was introduced to all the computer laboratories under ISTC. It is presently on a testing period and based on observation, the implementation was a success—attributed mainly to the smooth flow of users availing of the printing services in our laboratories.

The Prepaid Card is available in all the computer laboratories and can be acquired by simply following these steps:

  • Get payment slip from any lab.
  • Go to the Cashier at the Accounting Office and pay the amount (P30 for 24 pages).
  • Get both the white and yellow copy of the receipt and return to the lab.
  • Exchange the yellow copy of the receipt with properly validated Prepaid Card.

After which, one can now avail of the printing services of the labs. Take note, the Card is transferrable, which means that if you think 24 pages is too much, you can lend it to your friend and let him/her enjoy the printing privileges as well.

Should there be any questions/clarifications/concerns, just e-mail Helpdesk@mail.dlsu.edu.ph or call ISTC at local 316. We will be glad to entertain and answer your queries.

Glen Bertulfo 07/05/1999
