    CFO Updates
 

Merging of TINLIB and ISIS

The merging of TINLIB and ISIS into one server is almost complete. The purpose of the integration is to trim down the number of servers the Center is currently maintaining. Thanks to Ronald, Ricky and Albert.

 

 

Electronic CLASSLIST via DLSU web

Members of the faculty can now download the Electronic CLASSLIST thru the web; ACU is completing this project including the procedural guide to assist the interested faculty members in downloading the file.

 

 

Anti-Virus software Evaluation

Evaluation of the Anti-Virus software is still going on and is expected to be finished within the first week of October; Also being evaluated is a   help desk software and Novell Zen Works for software distribution and inventory.

 

 

Other Upgrades

LS-335 laboratory is now connected to the network.  Upgrading of LS-212, G-304A and G-304B to Pentium Celeron 400 with 64MB of memory and 8.4GB Hard Disk were completed last 3rd week of Sept. 1999.

On the otherhand, the OS and memory of all XYLAN Switches are also being upgraded.

 

 

New Juniors in the CFO clan

Please help us welcome the new PC/Network Engineers: Dominador Diokno Jr and Eduardo de los Reyes Jr.  CFO now have four (4) Juniors…Ricky Jr., Ricarte Jr., Dominador Jr., and Ed Jr.
