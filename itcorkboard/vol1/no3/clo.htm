CLO-SA Reunion Scheduled on December

Plans for the much awaited Alumni SAs Reunion had been initiated and had been going on for the past two months now. This is a joint project of the Central Committee and the Reunion Committee (composed of Student Assistants) for December and is inspired by the 15 years anniversary celebration of ISTC.

In this regard, several committees had been formed to accommodate the different needs of the activity. The proposed schedule of this activity was set to December 4, 1999 at 6pm and possible venues for this gathering include Century Park Sheraton Hotel Ballroom, Northwing of the Conservatory and Marian Quadrangle—costing and other financial related matters are currently being determined by the Budget Committee. Being one of the major concerns of the organizing committee, a Solicitations Committee had been activated to find other means and solutions to support the needed amount. The other committees, like the Programs Committee and Technical Committee, are assigned to handle the entertainment portions of the party.
