    DOCU Updates
 

New Additions to the DLSU Website

Have you been to the 2 new additions to our website? 

First is the DLSU Viewbook. As posted on the web page, it is a glimpse of the University in a nutshell. It gives the net surfers a quick view of the DLSU community.  The Viewbook, which is a marketing project of the MCO, has been online for the past 3 months now.

Likewise, the Inter-Institutional Consortium website is now online.  I-IC is composed of 5 schools where DLSU is one of them. Other members are: St. Paul's College-Manila, St. Scholastica's College, Philippine Christian College, and Philippine Normal University.  I-IC's primary objective is to mobilize the resources of the member schools to complement each other's strengths and areas of improvement.

 

Intranet Survey Results

The Intranet Survey conducted by the Docu Office last July 7, 1999 has already been tallied and evaluated.  Out of 1354 e-mail and 60 internal mail recipients, seventy-three (73) responded through the e-mail and four (4) responded through the internal mail for a total of seventy-seven (77) respondents (5.5% Response Percentage).  This survey was conducted in line with the reorganization/revision of the Intranet.  This was done primarily to know what kind of information the DLSU community wants out of the facility.  View the complete tally of the survey.

 

DLSU Colleges webpages

Updates from different colleges such as CLA and COS are starting to arrive in line with the revision and updating of the colleges web pages.  It has been years that these web pages remain untouched and detached to the look and feel of the DLSU-Manila web site.   The plan is to standardize the contents and create a nearly uniform layout.   Updates from other colleges are still welcome, please send them in. 

 

ISTC Handbook & JDQS Revision

After several outline revisions, the ISTC Operations Manual is now  completed.   The new Handbook contains more information which can help the ISTPs in their day-to-day dealings.  The different forms used by the Center are attached to the manual complete with fill-out instructions.  Likewise, the ISTC Implementation Guidelines is also attached to the manual for easy referencing.  And unlike the previous one, it only contains the general policies and procedures; those applicable only to a particular unit were decided to be placed in the handbook of that unit which will be one of the next projects to be spearheaded by the Docu Office.

Another pertinent ISTC documentation which was revised and will soon come your way is the Job Description and Qualification Standards (JDQS).  The terms and descriptions used in the manual were standardized thru the concerted efforts of the Director and Unit heads.  The new JDQS also has an enhanced layout for easy browsing and reading. 

Enjoy reading!

 

Joie Alvarez 10/15/1999
