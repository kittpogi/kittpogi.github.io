HOW TO SET YOUR MS WINDOWS DATE Y2K COMPLIANT

 1. Click on START button at the lower left corner of your computer screen.

2. From the Start menu, point to SETTINGS and click CONTROL PANEL.


3. Double-click on REGIONAL SETTINGS, the one with a globe icon.

 4. Click on the DATE tab at the top of the page.

5. Click on the drop down arrow across SHORT DATE STYLE and select the option that shows mm/dd/yyyy to have a four-digit year format.

6. Click on APPLY and then click on OK at the bottom.

 After you click Apply, the Short Date Sample area shows how Windows will display the date based on the settings in the Short Date Style and Date Separator list boxes.
