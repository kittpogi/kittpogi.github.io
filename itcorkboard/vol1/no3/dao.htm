    DAO Updates

 

File Migration

In line with the integration of the Student Records Management System, DAO has converted majority of the reference master files in VAX Rdb to Oracle.  Among those converted were the degree (generic and DLSU), department/college, country, region, district/province, position, fees, visa status, study grant, offenses, employee, assissment types, course and school.  The school file has just been cleaned of duplicates and data encoding errors.  Those with addresses have also been assigned the postal zip codes.  Data entry and query programs on the above masterfiles have been developed in cooperation with the SMO.

The Graduate student and CSB student databases have been converted to Oracle.  The conversion of the undergraduate DLSU Manila student databases is ongoing and expected to be completed within the term.

 

 

Data Modelling

The conceptual design of the Human Resources Data Model has been completed.  Documentation is on-going.  The conceptual design of the Facilities Data Model has also started.
