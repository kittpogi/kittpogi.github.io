The New DLSU Intranet

Tired of the DLSU Intranet? You can't find any new information whatsoever, or you haven't been visiting it lately. Well, be glad because for the past few months, the Web Development Team is currently reorganizing and redesigning the DLSU Intranet. You won't see any of those tired graphics anymore but some eye-popping effects and eye candy for you without sacrificing the true intent of the Intranet, that is to deliver relevant information when you want it, where you want it.

The new Intranet (as we put it for the meantime) will include all the information present in the old (the existing Intranet) plus some neat features. For starters, the Electronic Class Lists for the faculty can now be downloaded through the Intranet. A download section for cliparts, office forms and some freeware will be also present. Several search engines and CGI codes are also being evaluated to be used in the Intranet for more accurate indexing and searching of web pages. Other features to be included are the discussion board which is like a chat program where persons with similar interests on a particular subject can ask or trade for some information, another feature is the use of online forms (Online Vacation Leave Forms, Online Sick Leave Forms, Request for Repairs, Room Reservation, etc. ) which will reduce the use of paper and the document's progress can be tracked in real-time. You can also query the database on the number of days you have left for your Vacation Leave or Sick Leave, your Academic Records or even the status of your loans. You can also register online for various University events such as seminars, symposia, fora. There will be no falling in line this time (if you cared to register online, that is).

So you're asking where do we get all this ideas. Well, these ideas came from you. Do you remember the Intranet Survey Form we sent through the e-mail or internal mail? The result of that survey is the new DLSU Intranet. But that doesn't mean we will stop there. We will be putting an Online Survey and Suggestion Box in the new Intranet so that we can always know what you think of the Intranet and how we can improve it.

We'll leave it at that for the moment and anticipate for the "launching" of the new DLSU Intranet.
