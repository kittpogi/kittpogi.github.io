SITE Thesis Systems

SEMINAR ORGANIZER PLUS:

The Seminar Organizer Plus (SOP), a conference monitoring system, will help seminar organizers in handling seminar activities such as registration, confirmation (through email or phone), publicity (through the web), even the printing of name tags and certificates. To be housed at ISTC, the system will adapt a client-server architecture. In addition, the SOP can be accessed on-line and will be a web-based application.

 COURSE APPROVAL SYSTEM:

The DLSU Course Approval System (CAS) will be an on-line and automated version of the existing manual processes involved in course approval. Non-block students, vice deans and academic assistants will be the primary users of this project. The CAS will be developed in a web-based environment so that users can access it anytime and anywhere. It will cover the course approval activities of the whole university (with the exception of the College of Liberal Arts, due to the complexity of its procedures regarding course approval).
