The Busy Bodies

Hello I’m back!

I’m sure you’ll all agree that last term just passed by so swiftly. Care to know what kept the busy buddies so "fit"!?! Scroll down if you wish to.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Hmmm… Let me see, apart from the renovation that gave us our new assigned cubicle. The much-awaited Disbursement Review under Ms. Marivic and Ella is now about 99% finished. By this time, Ella will just be printing the documents of their project. After which, it will be coursed through to the ISTC – Director then to the EVP for approval and for implementation. Watch out for the release of this new convenient process. By the way, I forgot to tell you I got the biggest cubicle (don’t ask why the biggest okay!?!).

How about Amy and me? Well, our Undergraduate Pre-enrollment Review is smooth sailing. The streamlining and standardization of the enrollment procedure was just agreed upon by our PRIT members. What will our students be expecting of this streamlined procedure!? One of course is the computerization of CAPEFs (hopefully this will be piloted by the College of Science this coming School Year) and favorably the short queues per transaction. Amy, Ella, and I joined forces in pursuing the next phase of the project that is the Presentation of the Proposed Recommendations. Our succeeding meetings with our PRIT members will cover their time table assignments. Of course, I would like to commend Sir Sam and Brian during PRIT meetings for assisting and relaying assignments to the offices concerned.

Can’t get enough of us? Here’s more…Ella attended a two day training of Internal Quality Auditing at Shangri - La Plaza last August and recently finished her echo training to a few number of ISTC attendees (good work!). Amy on the other hand, attended a weeklong training for the Lead Auditor/Auditor also at Shangri – La Plaza last August. She might give her echo training this month… Attendees be prepared. In addition to this training, she attended another one, which was that of the TQM at La Salle Greenhills that lasted for a month and half! (No wonder why she’s our "fit" lady!).

I just remembered CFO, how was your IQA? Which office is next… let’s all wait and be ready for it. Ella will be giving tips to help you with your IQA. Want to know what these are!?! Well, she just started her research on benchmarking of Good House Keeping on Seven S and Quality Improvement Tools, Practices and Programs. Learn more about it on her article.

 

That’s it for now… see yah on our next issue.

 

 

Eileen Manabat 10/15/1999
