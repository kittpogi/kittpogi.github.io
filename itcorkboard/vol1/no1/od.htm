Negotiations for the New PSI Faculty Manual
The negotiation for the revision of the PSI Faculty Manual is ongoing. The Techincal Panel consists of the representatives from the College of Computer Studies, Information Systems and Technology Center and Graduate School of Business, and administrators of the Professional Schools Inc. It is now on its fifth meeting since December last year. The revisions for the manual include additional positions, additional benefits, changes in employment norms and conditions, and changes in board compositions, among others. Celine V. Rivera 02/19/1999

ISTP Organizational Movements

Nick Bonao is now the Coordinator for the Computer Facilities and Operations Office (CFO). He has assumed the position when the former Coordinator, Julius Suarez went on professional leave effective January this year. Glen Bertulfo, a newly hired ISTP, is now the Acting Coordinator for Computer Laboratory Operations (CLO), the position vacated by Nick. Recruitment is ongoing for existing vacancies at CFO, Process Review and Improvement Office, Research and Development Office and Documentation Office. (Refer to ISTC job opportunities at URL http://www.dlsu.edu.ph/offices/istc/job.htm)
Celine V. Rivera 02/19/1999
