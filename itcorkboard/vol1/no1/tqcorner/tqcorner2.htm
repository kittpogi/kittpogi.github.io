   The TQ Corner


Some tips to improve your telephone skills:

Replace

With
"I’ll have to...." "I’ll be happy to...."
"I’ll try...." "I will...."
"But...." "And...."
"I’m sorry you...." "Thank you for calling it to our attention."
"Can I help you?" "May I help you?"
"Who’s calling?" "May I ask who’s calling?"
"She’s on her coffee break." "She’s away from her desk at the moment."
"He isn’t in yet." (in the morning) "He’s not at his desk at the moment."
"For a while..." "Please hold your line."

Remember, the idea is to always convey the best possible image.
