   The TQ Corner


Welcome to the TQ Corner! This corner was created to have a venue to discuss tips on how to improve our skills and services, to discuss the latest on Total Quality Management (TQM), or the latest on Business Process Improvement (BPI), Kaizen, etc. You’re even welcome to contribute. We hope you’ll enjoy and get inputs from our little column. We also hope it somehow contributes to the realization of DLSU 2000, of becoming a world-class university.

Let’s start with time management. Here are some tips to finding more time in your day:

  • Be honest with your time.
  • Don’t promise more than you know you can complete in the time allotted.
  • Find a single daily planning system that works for you.
  • Drop low priority tasks from your list.
  • Take a re-energizing mini break during your day.

Remember, we all have tons and tons of work to do yet we only have 24 hours a day. So, find more time in your day.
