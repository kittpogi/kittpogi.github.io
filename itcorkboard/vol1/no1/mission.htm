ISTC Mission-Vision

The DLS-PSI Information Systems & Technology Center sees itself to be a highly effective proactive team of committed and motivated Christian Achievers. It is guided by the values of Religio, Mores, and Cultura.

It spearheads the pervasive application of information technology with excellent service, innovative leadership, holistic human resource development, continual reengineering, effective change management, and quality assurance.

It envisions itself to be involved with cross-functional and cross-organizational teams in Lasallian and world-class communities.



ISTC Quality Policy

In our pursuit of the DLSU mission-vision, we are committed to contribute and participate in ensuring and improving quality of services to the delight of our customers.
