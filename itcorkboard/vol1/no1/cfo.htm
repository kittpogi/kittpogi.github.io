    CFO Updates
 

E1 Link from DLSU to MOSCOM

CFO is currently into the testing phase of the new E1 line provided by Mosaic Communications. This line is intended to replace the current 128Kbps uplink of IPHIL. The new E1 link will provide full 2Mbps bandwidth to the campus as an attempt to solve the current bandwidth problem, as well as provide for future needs.

Upgrade to OPENVMS 7.1 and ORACLE 8

The twin upgrades to OpenVMS 7.1 and Oracle 8 were completed last week. Oracle 8 will enable a set of tools ranging from rapid application to financial analysis.

Y2K Certification

We are requiring all our suppliers for software and hardware, existing and to be purchased, to furnish us a Certificate of Y2K compliance. This is to ensure that we will be ready for the millennium bug.

A new addition to the CFO family

We would like to welcome Patricia, the baby girl of Ma. Lourdes Bernadette Ibay, who was born on February 19, 1999. Congratulations to the new parents.

Nick Bonao 02/23/1999
