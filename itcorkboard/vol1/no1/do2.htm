What's New On DLS System Page
The DLS System home page (http://system.dlsu.edu.ph) has a new design. It has incorporated the home page of the Professional Schools Inc. The previously white background now has a dark shade of green. The pictures are rearranged from a circular format to alternating left-right vertical format. The President’s page now an announcement area where memos from the DLSU President will be posted. In addition to this, the home pages of the DLSU Dasmarinas and DLSU Zobel are now in their own servers. The DLSU-Dasmarinas is at http://www.dasma.dlsu.edu.ph and the DLSU Zobel is at http://www.zobel.dlsu.edu.ph. These two home pages are also sporting new designs.

Come and visit the page to see what’s new with our system schools.
Celine V. Rivera 02/19/1999
