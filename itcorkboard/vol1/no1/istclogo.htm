    The ISTC

The ISTC Identity

ISTC has designed itself a new logo to graphically represent the holistic profile of the center. The logo has undergone five proposals before the final design landed in official ISTC documents. The logomark is a combination of 5 elements, each standing for ISTC’s commitment to service.

The main element is the acronym ISTC – which of course stands for "Information Systems & Technology Center".

The five-pointed star in gold at the upper left-corner of the acronym is the Signum Fidei star of DLSU. It is the sign of faith and is the symbol of the La Salle Brothers (Fraternum Scholarum Christianarum). It symbolizes ISTC’s commitment to the La Sallian values Religio, Mores and Cultura. The star also means hope.

The radial lines inside the text signifiy ISTC’s vision to shine and to be involved with cross-functional and cross organizational teams in the DLS System and other educational communities. Graphically, it gives an illusion of movement that suggests vitality in service.

The clockwise rotary orbit (perspective) in green-dark-green graduated tone illustrates that of a computer network around the DLSU systems, community, and the world with a high-speed information system at work. It also suggests a revolutionary in information technology and communication advancement through worldwide networking.

The slanting concept of the acronym ‘ISTC’ into an oval shape orbit (as if it were high up in space) gives it a more dynamic profile – simulates the feeling of ascent; summarizing a fast and reliable service, management and human resource development towards the next millennium.

Each ISTC staff should put into life the symbolism of the ISTC seal, for it is only through our quality service that these elements speak true of its meaning.

Joie B. Alvarez, Lemuel B. Cabia 02/18/1999
