New Babies of SMO

SMO warmly welcomes Ma. Agnes C. Bulay and Johnny Benitez, Jr. who were hired as Jr. Programmers last December 1, 1999 and January 3, 2000, respectively.  Agnes will team up with Harold Paulo Bautista forming the new ‘adhoc’ team.  Meantime, Johnny will team up with  ‘Fearless Integrated Students Record Management  (ISRM) Team’ or FIT.

ETP - Pasicatchan Winners

“Pasicatchan ‘99” a much-awaited event in celebration of CCS day was held at William Shaw Mini Theatre on November 26, 1999 in which some of the SITE students participated and won in the 3 popular categories.  In the band category, the band of Victor John Abrenica , Manuel Martinez, and Paul Ryan Tiburcio won the 1st prize.  Victor John played the lead ,  Manuel  played bass, while Paul Ryan was on vocals.  The trio was also in the group which won the 1st prize in the group singing category.   On the other hand, Irvin Alzona, Rodrigo Gajitos, Jr., and Manuel Martinez were in the group who won the 2nd prize in the spoof category. You’ve proven anew your versatility. Keep it up guys!

Lichelle Legutan 02/11/2000
