    DAO Updates


File Migration

In preparation for the Integrated Student Records Management System, the Student File of the undergraduate students and the Transcript File of College of St. Benilde students have been migrated to Oracle from VAX Rdb.  These are in addition to the files migrated last term.  The Transcript File for the graduate school students will follow after completion of the grades encoding by the Registrar’s Office.

Guidelines for Query on Reference Files

Documentation of the guidelines for query on selected reference files is ongoing.  The query facility has been provided to ensure that the users, namely: Registrar’s Office, Admissions Office and CSB Registrar’s Office have access to updated data.  This will also minimize the need to request for the printing of the reference files masterlist.

DAO Acquires Masterfile of School Addresses

Upon request from the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS), DAO has acquired a file of the names and addresses of the entire primary and secondary schools in the Philippines as of 1999.  This will greatly facilitate the verification of the Philippine schools’ addresses requested to be added to our masterfile.  This will also enable the assignment of exact school codes and zip codes for the schools.
