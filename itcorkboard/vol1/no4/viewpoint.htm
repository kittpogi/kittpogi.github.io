Encore: Online Freshmen Entrance Exam Results Query

In contrast to the last year's insensible view on this subject, I'm a little sympathetic now.  As I read through the various e-mails from the inquirers, I can see a cloud of anxiety at their end.  Entering college is really a gruesome experience: new environment, long queues, sense of uncertainty and of course, a big deal of entrance exams.  It has been pressure and stress from start to finish.  Just imagine how the hearts of these applicants start pounding as they access the DLSU web site, and much audible poundings as they click the search button only to read the phrase "Search not found."

On that account, I confidently reckon that the violent and demanding reactions e-mailed to the Webmaster are more of worry and concern on their application status and not inefficiency on our part.  They are simply worried.  We just have to understand.  Read for yourself….
