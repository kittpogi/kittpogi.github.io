DLSU acquires satellite dish

DLSU has recently acquired a satellite dish to facilitate Internet access via satellite with guaranteed bandwidth of 512 kbps to a maximum of 4Mbps direct connection with the US-based AboveNet (Palo Alto Inet Exchange). This direct connection will augment our existing E1 connection to Mosaic Communications (MosCom) Philippines.  A private firm has been subcontracted for the installation, after which, ISTC, together with MosCom representatives will test the connection.  The satellite dish will be installed at the rooftop of the Gokongwei Building by February 2000.
 

Good Housekeeping Committee

The ISTC, through the newly-formed Good Housekeeping Committee, will be implementing the concept of 5S, a management technique applied by the Japanese with the emphasis on the cleanliness and orderliness in the workplace. The Good Housekeeping Committee is composed of one representative from every ISTC unit and is headed by Ms. Ella Calaor (PRIO). The Committee was formally introduced during the ISTC Staff Meeting held on January 24, 2000 where the Committee's acting spokesperson Mr. Glen D. Bertulfo (CFO/CLO) presented the 5S concept to the ISTP. The Committee adopted the theme "Sama-Sama Sa Simpleng Sistema", which will be used by the Center for its 5S activities. A poster making contest was organized for the different units as the first activity for the 5S cause (results inside).
