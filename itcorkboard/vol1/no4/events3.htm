DLSU-CREM Training Programs

Module 1: Effective Media Technology Application

Focus on the familiarization of the various types of instructional technologies used in the classroom. Participants will be introduced to the basic procedures in selecting, modifying and designing technology-based learning tools. Stress is made on transparency-making and the proper use of the overhead  projector for classroom presentation.

  • ISTC Atendees: Harold Paolo D. Bautista, Mary Jean B. Soria, Patricia B. Antonio, Glen D. Bertulfo, Vivian C. Sy , Joel B. Kisteria, Daisy T. Binuya, Joie B. Alvarez, Catalina M. Quiogue, Ma. Lourdes M. Ibay, Arfeli B. Balanza, Rowena A. Santos

Module 2: Powerpoint in the Classroom (two days)

Focus on the computer technology as a presentation tool. Emphasis is made on the basic application of the Powerpoint Program in class presentations.

  • Attendees: Ma. Ella B. Calaor, Amalia D. Salcedo, Glen D. Bertulfo, Eileen D. Manabat, Loveleah V. Malig, Patricia B. Antonio, Daisy T. Binuya, Joel B. Kisteria, Joie B. Alvarez, Catalina M. Quiogue, Brian O. Co, Ma. Lourdes M. Ibay, Arfeli B. Balanza

Module 3: Designing a Basic Interactive Multimedia Presentation (two days)

Provide the attendees with the basics of design of interactive materials and familiarization with the internet. Participants will use the Hyperstudio application to create a simple interactive program. Participants will also learn Photoshop and the basics of Website Designs.

  • Attendees: Amalia D. Salcedo, Ma. Ella B. Calaor, Eileen D. Manabat, Glen D. Bertulfo, Patricia B. Antonio, Daisy T. Binuya, Joel B. Kisteria, Joie B. Alvarez, Ma. Lourdes M. Ibay, Arfeli B. Balanza

Daisy Binuya, 02/11/2000
