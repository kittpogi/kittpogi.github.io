Outreach Activity '99

Christmas is a time for sharing and it came early to ISTC during the Outreach activity to Asocacion de Damas Filipinas in November 27, 1999. About forty children were blessed by the generosity of the ISTC people who gave their time, effort and money for the cause. What is Christmas without children? Isn’t it that the Lord came to us as a humble child to bring us salvation? The innocence of children amuses us, but more often than not, their antics annoy us. But they are children, and we were once too. Seeing a child’s eye open wide in wonder after receiving a gift makes the giver proud. It is the same feeling that we made our ninong and ninang happy when they find we liked the gifts they gave us when we were kids.

Sharing is not new to ISTC people. From the pasalubong that one bring after coming back from the province or coming back from a well-deserved vacation, or from the thoughtful goodies that one shares with the whole office, it is the spirit of giving is the tie that binds us. From this activity conceived by the Team Building committee, we bring ourselves a notch higher and closer to each other.

Kudos to the organizers, Team Building committee and to the different committees during the day itself (especially the Food Committee for leaving us something we can share with the rest of the office) for a job well done. Recognition should be given to the master of ceremonies, Tito Pepe himself… Mr. Glen Bertulfo of CFO and to the CFO storytellers Mr. Ed delos Reyes, Mr. Jun Diokno and Mr. Jhun Galang. Another wave of thanks should go to Mr. Ed delos Reyes for the transportation. The trip back to La Salle was a real "bonding" experience.

Francis Mark D. Santos, 02/10/1999
