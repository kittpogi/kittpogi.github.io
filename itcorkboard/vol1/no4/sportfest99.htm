Sportfest '99

ISTC took part in Palakasan ’99, a yearly gathering of faculty from the different colleges to compete in different sporting events. ISTC competed in the following events: Women’s and Men’s Volleyball and Men’s Basketball.  

The Women’s Volleyball Team was able to reach the crossover semifinals and they eventually placed second overall. The Men’s Volleyball and Basketball teams suffered from early setbacks and weren’t as lucky as the ladies to reach the semis. We’re hoping to field a better team this year to get another shot in grabbing that elusive championship.

Francis Mark D. Santos 02/10/1999
