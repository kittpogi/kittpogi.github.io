Instantly Add video to your phone

Beamer™, the world's first Phone Video Station™ (PVS) enables consumers to instantly add video to their phone calls, using their existing phone and phone line.

  • Works with any home phone (corded or cordless).
  • Works with any standard (analog) phone line.
  • No special wiring or equipment
  • No service fees, contracts or subscriptions
  • No additional cost to your phone call.

Performance Features
Beamer offers three viewing options: a snapshot/privacy option, the ability to select from a range of detail/movement settings, and a video confirmation button.

  • Three viewing choices - Self-view, Other party view, or Combo view.
  • Snapshot/Privacy option - send a high resolution video image and temporarily halt any new video transmission, until you want it to continue.
  • Detail/movement control to select the degree of definition.
  • Supports Caller ID (if you have this with your local phone service provider).
  • Video confirmation - no video is sent until you approve transmission.

Windows Tip: Watch DivX Movies on Your Windows Media Player

DivX is a compression standard based on MPEG-4 that allows video to be compressed to a fraction of its former size. A lot of DivX video is traded on the Internet due to its manageable size, but you can't play it back in a player without the free codec from DivXNetworks.

Download the free 3MB DivX package. (Don't download the ad-supported DivX Pro. You don't need all the extras for playback.) This package comes with a free DivX player, but when you install the package you can choose to install only the codec you need to play back DivX in your Windows Media Player. You don't have to play DivX in the DivX player.

Once this codec is installed, you can play DivX movies in your Windows Media Player.
