Secured PC Config Unleashed

As of press time, the computer labs under the ITC are undergoing a major change in security features. Together with the unrelentless team of lab assistants, we have discovered a way to totally secure Win2k PCs.

With this added feature in the labs, students are now restricted from changing the wallpaper setup, from changing the proxy settings and even from accessing system files.
Unfortunately, we cannot disclose yet the application responsible for such changes, we shall be glad however to show you a demo on the next staff meeting. That is, if you allow us to.

For now, let's just enjoy the secure and friendly environment of our computer facilities. Rest assured we will not rest until things get better within our jurisdiction. We only hope that you continue to trust us and support our actions.

Talk about changes…

We have recently setup five computer labs with their own mounted multimedia projectors. This is in line with our vision to provide quality services to our customers and in so doing they are now privileged to avail these facilities for free inside our computer labs.

Slowly but surely, we will continue to equip our facilities with devices that will make the lives of our customers simple and convenient (not to mention, interesting). Today, it will just be a simple multimedia system, tomorrow, it will be something far more advance.
