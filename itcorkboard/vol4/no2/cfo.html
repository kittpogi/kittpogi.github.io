Computer Facilities and Operations : Updates

CFO Events
 By:Samuel P. Mallare

  • The network has been re-configured to have virtual LANs (or VLANs). This will make our network "faster" and relatively more secure. Thanks to the help of 3com and Microwarehouse! Thanks also to Ronald and Noel who stayed overnight over the weekend!

  • We are reviewing our operations and focusing on urgent matters resulting from lapses in our processes.

    We are re-assigning server functions to attain a better set-up, which should result to better services, especially to SMO and DAO. Expect these adjustments to be completed within October and November. We just hope that we will not have problems with the software packages that we plan to consolidate on the server level. We have just ordered two more branded servers. These will incrementally improve the quality and reliability of CFO services.

  • We have been conducting several sessions to teach our customers on the use of the new e-mail system. Thanks to the Help Desk team!

  • Though it has been months since we have started with the e-mail migration from Pegasus Mail (netware6) to MS Outlook Web Access (OWA) (mail1), the most critical and high profile project now is the migration of file services from netware6 to mail1. We need the entire CFO team to coordinate very well for this to be successful.

  • The rest of the school year will be spent on improving CFO operations and services. We expect to have a recognizable improvement before the end of the school year.

  • While we are waiting for the delivery of MS SharePoint portal server, we're downloading related files from the Microsoft web sites so we can start "playing" around with the portal. CFO will install the evaluation copy and SMO will start developing web parts (i.e., portlets, in Java lingo). After more than three years of waiting, we will soon have a fully functional portal package for us to use. We can focus on developing information services and let MS SharePoint handle the underlying infrastructure and other knowledge management services (e.g., search engine and discussion boards). By (or before?) the end of next school year, the portal should be mature enough to have positive impact on major processes in the University.

  • We are hiring three people for our CFO PC/Network team. One of them (Mon) already reported for work but has not yet rendered his piece. Maybe in the next ITC meeting. The 2nd one (Lloyd) will report on October 24 2002. The 3rd one is Boyet, one of CLO's Lab Assistants. We're only waiting for the Hiring Board to convene for Boyet's application. Should Boyet still render a song for us?
