Office of the Director : Updates
by: Samuel P. Mallare

  • We will be putting copyright notices in our programs as we do in our web pages. It would be good to integrate that inside the program codes as well. This will enhance our awareness on copyright and IPR, not to mention better IPR protection.

  • We have some new contractual Lab Assistants to help CLO cope with the increasing manpower requirements of the computer labs.

  • We will be hiring a new CFO Department Head (Irwin). He will report on November 4, 2002. I look forward to better days for CFO.

  • We are still processing some applications for SMO and Docu.

  • We will have a new DLS System President next school year. Let's just wait for the official announcement. Let's pray for and thank Bro. Roly for his service, accomplishments, and dedication to DLSU.

  • We have started with the series of sessions on stress management. There were about 20 participants. I wonder how many will attend the future sessions. In my opinion, the key to successful "stress management" is the identification of 1) real and perceived stress sources and 2) current and alternative effective responses to stress.

  • The Department Heads are identifying people to our various performance objectives. Those assigned to monitor the performance objectives will serve as champions to those performance objectives. Each person will (among other things) find ways of improving measurement methods and re-assessing the related processes for us to achieve, if not surpass, our performance objectives. There might even be performance objectives, which we should redefine, replace, or remove. This will enhance our quality improvement efforts and, hopefully, positively contribute to the achievement of our vision. I look forward to your support and participation.

  • The documentation for our work plan is being completed - thanks to Docu! This is a major improvement compared to those in previous years. The focus was on operational concerns - i.e., "firefighting". This plan will be used more and more to see how we move forward with our projects. I look forward to better project management in all ITC units with the help of our work plan.

    Next year's work plan will be a bit different. The focus for next planning workshop will be the vision of ITC and how each unit will proactively contribute to achieve it. We have a picture of it. We have bits and pieces of it in various documents. We have discussed it in several formal and informal sessions and in varying degrees. It is time to lay down a more detailed picture of our plans to achieve our vision…It is not so far away! (Excluding courseware, of course!)

  • There might be changes in direction regarding our support services to other La Salle schools. Things are fuzzy for now. We will just wait for the decision of the administration.
