Teambuilding Committee : Updates

For every end, there is a beginning
 By: Joel G. Kisteria

As the SY 0102 just finished, here we are starting SY 0203. Funny how time is becoming too fast nowadays, or perhaps it's just being slow J

And for our TEAM BUILDING activities, here is what we have managed from the 3rd term of SY 0102 'til summer…

SPORTSmania… the ITP-athletes showed their Olympic form in the last DLSU-System sportsfest. And compared to last year, WE progressed. Not because of the milk, but because WE just did J… like the old saying goes "practice, practice, and more practice". One major sports breakthrough was the re-emergence of the sexy lady "ampel"... just ask her how she got the victories in the game called "ping pong". The volleyball girls, WOW! they were a revelation! Now the competition will be in for a surprise when it faces the team next season, huh! ...as long as there will be more practice… more food…

For the TGIH/TGIF aficionados, sorry guys, I believe we have to find a way to revive this. Since we all kinda got busy, this event went to the back seat. Missed it? We do! The food, the free movies, the voices when we sing free videoke, the games, the cool guys and gals. Everything! BUT THE NEW TEAM SEEM TO BE PROMISING… just watch out, ok?

The ITC CHOIR… well, if you happen to attend the daily masses here at the University, you probably have heard them a lot. Just check their booking schedules, you'll find out they are e-x-p-a-n-d-i-n-g their domain… they're even being invited to be instrumentalists outside of their singing schedule. And we'll still be hearing a lot from them… keep on praising!

SUMMER '02, no this ain't another love song… just something to write about the summer getaway of the ITPs, El Caseron, La Union… they said beach was good and so was the food. I wasn't there, so I don't think I can say a lot, except that I want to say Great Job to the Summer Committee!!! And for those who weren't there like me, just see the smiling faces and enjoyment in the pix.

Philippine General Hospital, yeah, that's where the TEAM went for the outreach, last 13 June 02. In coordination with DLSU-COSCA, we were able to give cash donation, and a little meryenda for a good number of indigent patients. No program activity was held because of the condition of the patients there. Thanks again to Sir Sam for facilitating the release of majority of the cash donation; Rhaniel and Sir James and for all those who helped with the crushing and disposal of the cans, guys/gals you know who you are J.

And finally, the NEWBIES (The NEW TEAM)… let's all welcome, Trish, Ryan, Francis (Kitt), Precious!!! They will be taking over now… It's closing time now for L Daisy, (Johnny - he left us already), Albert, and that KIDD called Joel.

It has been a wonderful TIME…

Be cool y'all
