ASAP Fever Hits CLO
 By: Glen D. Bertulfo

The stars of the Sunday favorite noon time show is now part of the CLO group. That’s right folks, meet the new hunks of the CLO group, Randy, Mark and Marvin. They will be joining the elite group of lab assistants starting this school year bringing the total number of eligible bachelors in CLO to seven (7) — guess who the seventh guy is?

Among others, the three have a special liking for rap music as evidenced by their stunning performances in the regular ITC staff meetings and are into playing basketball. CLO is now a stronger force to reckon with in the next ITC basketball events, something to watch out for in the near future.

With their profound skills in computer hardware and software maintenance just like Jeff, Boyet and Garry, they are expected to bring in more improvements and quicker response time to the demands in the computer labs. They are assigned to the following facilities respectively; MSCL, G404 and V301 and if the need arises, they will be assigned to other labs as well to make our facilities 100% available.

By the way, watch out for their upcoming concert slated sometime before Christmas entitled What’s Going On??! A Rap Concert. Just watch out for print ads for details, okay? Until next time.

Game K N B mag-CIS?
By: Glen D. Bertulfo

Nagsimula na noong ika-3 ng Hunyo, 2002 ang sistemang binuo ng SMO para sa kapakanan ng CLO Student Assistants. Ang tawag sa programang ito ay Computer Lab Information System o CIS. Ito ay binuo sa pamumuno ni Bb. Arfeli Balanza at ang kanyang escort na si Mark Rufino. Sa programang ginawa ni Mark Rufino, ang dating manu-manong trabaho ng mga SAs sa laboratoryo ay mapapadali at magiging simple.

Halimbawa na lamang ay ang paga-apdeyt ng mga estudyanteng gumagamit sa lab, awtomatiko na ia-assign ng programa kung saan sila uupo at kung anong gagamitin nilang kompyuter. Pati na rin ang pag-kompyut ng mga allowance ng SAs ay mabilis na ring makukuha sa pamamagitan ng sistemang ito.

Malaking pasasalamat ang ipinaaabot ng CLO sa SMO sa kanilang suportang ipinakita dito. Umaasang hindi lamang dito nagtatapos ang kanilang tulong bagkus sana ay magkaroon pa ng ibang programa na maaaring ma-develop para lalong ma-improve ang sistema sa ating mga pasilidad.
