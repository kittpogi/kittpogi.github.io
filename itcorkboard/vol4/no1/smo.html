ORACLE Financials Implementation Making Great Strides
By: Brian O. Co

Phase three of the Oracle Financials project is proceeding smoothly. Phase three includes the Inventory, Fixed Assets, Purchasing, and Cash Management modules.

Full implementation of all these modules is scheduled sometime September 2002 for the Manila campus and December 2002 for the other system schools (including the College of Saint Benilde (CSB), Dasmariñas, Health Sciences Campus (HSC), and Zobel).

Needless to say, Accounting Office personnel led by Ms. Raquel Bondoc, University Controller, contributed greatly to the progress made in implementing the system; however, the project has actually crossed functional and organizational lines as it is the combined efforts of people from all the DLS System Schools, specifically from the Central Purchasing Station (CPS), Information Technology Center (ITC), Internal Audit, Warehouse and Inventory Management Office (WIMO), Purchasing Office, and of course, our partners at Oracle.

There were several bumps in the road such as unforeseen system bugs, network problems, and even the lack of a venue. But, there is not a problem so big that honest dialogue and teamwork cannot resolve.

Online Application for Change of Grade, Already Up
 By: Lorna P. Acorda

The Online Change of Grade Application is one of the forms from the Office of the University Registrar that is made available online to provide faculty members with a more convenient means to access the form. Faculty members may use any Internet-connected computer on or off-campus to access the form.

The introduction of this online form is consistent with the University's goal of computerization and the continuous improvement of its services.

To get to the Application for Change of Grade page, open any Internet browser e.g., Microsoft Internet Explorer or Netscape Communicator and type the following URL: http://www.dlsu.edu.ph/change

The login page will appear first. Faculty members will log-in using their ID number and birthday. The Application for Change of Grade page should appear. The faculty member will then be asked to enter the information needed in the form. When all the information are filled-out, a form in portable document format (PDF) will then load and the information entered will be placed in the Application for Change of Grade form. This can be printed out and later on submitted to the Office of the University Registrar for further processing.

Faculty members who receive an "Invalid ID"/"Invalid Birthday" message, but are sure that they have entered their ID number and birthday correctly should contact Nena Santos of the EVP Office through local 256.

There are three classifications of the Change of Grade form: (1) Thesis/Practicum, (2) Non-Thesis and (3) Beyond the Deadline. A written letter addressed to the University Registrar is required as attachment to the form if the application for Change of Grade is the classification "Beyond the Deadline".

The SMART card
 By: Ryan Q. Salvanera

The transition from the old bar-coded card to the new SMART card began on July 1, 2002.

The students are no longer bothered in taking out their ID cards every time they pass the gates. They can no longer use the long queues as an excuse for being late in class. In the future, with the "contact-less" card, they will be able to do cashless transactions, be it paying for food at the cafeteria, for transcripts at the Office of the University Registrar, paying overdue fines at the Library, paying parking fees or even buying stuff at the bookstore.

Inspired by the ITC's mission of providing quality IT services and resources, the SMO Team tried to meet the target implementation date amidst a number of sleepless nights, headaches and other difficulties.

Extracurricular Information System (XIS)
 By: Brian O. Co

Here is an overview of the Extracurricular Information System (XIS), a popular module in the My.LaSalle (MLS) portal

  1. Student Information Update
    Update of information on personal details, guardian information, health concerns, accomplishments, academic data, and extracurricular involvement (internal and external to DLSU)
    Output: Prospective list (based on freshmen's high school extracurricular involvements), Invitation to join organization
  2. Management of Organizations
    • Membership Maintenance
      Granting of access rights, definition of organization-specific questions, application acceptance, automatic renewal of membership (subject to organizational requirements), review of scholarship status,
    • Recruitment
      Submission of applicant's personal information, extracurricular participation, and answers to organization-specific questions
    • Attendance Monitoring
      Monitoring of member's participation in activities
    • Performance and Status Supervision
      Monitoring of members' performance in terms of attendance and compliance to requirements
      Outputs: List of Active/Inactive Members, Statistics and Graphs
  3. Student Org Application
    Application to student organizations
  4. Private Access Calendar
    Posting of campus events and activities
    Note: Public events will be handled by the Campus Event Organizer (CEO) system
  5. Decision Support System
    • Performance and Status Supervision
      Monitoring of organizations and the entire student body in terms of extracurricular participation and the organizations' management of members' activities
    • Search Engine
      Selection of student representatives based on certain criteria (g.p.a., participation in extracurricular activities, etc.)
      Output: Statistics and Graphs
  6. XIS Resume generation
    Summarized version of student's personal info and extracurricular participation
