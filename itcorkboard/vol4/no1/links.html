Cable TV Invades the Web

AXN Action TV
www. axn-asia.com

Sony Pictures Entertainment, maker of such films as Men In Black, Godzilla and The Mask of Zorro, researched the popularity of action and adventure entertainment, commonly believed to be the most popular entertainment genre in Asia. And what did the results say? ACTION ranks as the #1 entertainment genre, not only in Asia, but across the globe. ACTION is #1, not only in cinemas, but also in video rentals and sales, on television, in video games, in sports, and other mediums. This information resulted in the world’s first dedicated action and adventure television network — AXN ACTION TV — on 21 September, 1997.

Discovery Channel
dsc.discovery.com

Since Discovery Channel first went on the air in 1985, it has grown to be the leading global real world media anf entertainment company.

HBO Asia
www.hboasia.com

Launched in 1992, Home Box Office (HBO) is a 24-hour subscription movie channel. It offers viewers in Asia a choice of more than 70 commercial-free movies every month, of which at least 28 are new. About 10% of HBO programming is compiled of exclusive entertainment specials, namely music shows, series, comedies and documentaries.

MTV Asia
www.mtvasia.com

MTVAsia.com was officially launched by MTV Networks Asia and Asiacontent.com in May 1999, and is designed to be the No.1 music and entertainment destination on the World Wide Web for young Asians. MTVAsia.com is updated daily, and is divided into international and regional sections (Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, and Indonesia). MTV fans can get round-the-clock access to information on the Network's most popular programs and concepts, including music charts, the MTV Artist of the Month, and the MTV Hot Seat segments. MTV's VJs are also featured on the site, in addition to information about MTV-branded consumer products, including album compilations, pagers, and clothing.

Star TV
www.startv.com

Launched in 1991 with five television channels, STAR pioneered satellite television in Asia and in the process catalysed explosive growth in the media industry across the entire region. Today STAR broadcasts 40 services in eight languages and offers a comprehensive choice of entertainment, sports, movies, music, news and documentaries.

Tech TV
techtv.com

TechTV is the 24-hour cable television channel about computing, technology, and the Internet. Devoted to providing its viewers the latest on the digital lifestyle, TechTV produces original television programming with simultaneous content on its fully integrated companion website, TechTV.com.
