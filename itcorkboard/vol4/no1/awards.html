A Plateau of Excellence
 By: Florinda D. Tamonan

They say the only difference between ordinary and extra-ordinary is that little "extra". And this school year, we recognize those ITPs who made that "extra" difference from the rest based on their performance last school year.

Excellence Award is given on the basis of the ITEO evaluation, peer nomination and attendance. Each honored ITP is given a plaque, a monetary award or a certificate.

This year, we give commendation to those whose passion for work is uncompromising. They are the ones who believe that service is an integral part of continued success and their number one goal is service excellence.

For two consecutive years, his commitment to excellence goes beyond expectation, by providing personalized service to others. Perhaps the reason why he maintains his plateau of excellence award is his unbridled passion for work and his character shaped by his moral values and personality. And now, for his third consecutive year, the Excellence Award for SY 2001-2002 is given to Rhaniel Bingcang. Jhun Diokno and Jhun Galang are chosen as runners-up for this Award of Excellence.

Jean Soria, for her second consecutive year, exemplifies remarkable discipline in attendance as well. Her plateau of excellence in terms of perfect attendance will for sure leave a mark that will serve as a model to every ITP. The same award is also given to Jhun Galang.
