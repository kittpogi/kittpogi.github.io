DAO: UPDATES

Research Data Model

The design of the Research Data Model based on the University Research Coordinating Office is ongoing. Initial presentation has been done. The model will cover both faculty and student researches. However, as of now, only the requirements for faculty research have been considered. As the study is completed, the data model will be expanded. Output from the study will be used by the Systems Management Office for system development.

Integrated Department File

The integrated department file was designed to create a single file of department codes that will be used by all systems that need the department data. This will facilitate consistency and integrity. Once the old and new codes have been mapped, the file will be made available to SMO to be integrated in their systems. The department file will be further expanded to include the names of head of the departments, secretary and local number. The codes will also be evaluated by the Computer Facilities and Operations Office if these can be used as basis for the email account/user name file.

Cost Estimation Model Report

The Cost Estimation Model for school year 2001-2002 has been completed. The study measures the cost of educating a student of a particular college for a term.. It shows how much the University spends or earns per degree and shows which program contributes significantly to the cost and revenue. Sources of data include the SRM, audited financial reports, payroll and faculty plantilla. The DAO has automated the generation of the report.

The results of the study has been presented to the EVP, Finance Director and VPAR but will still be presented to the Operations Council, Council of Deans and Council of Chairs for information dissemination and policy formulation.
