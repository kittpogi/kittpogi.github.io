Office of the Director : Updates
by: Samuel P. Mallare

  • ICT REQUIREMENTS - We will coordinate with the DLSU-Canlubang team on their ICT requirements. We will provide them with our admissions, SRM, library, and accounting (FAS) systems initially. The use of our SRM will be a key challenge as it will be an implementation of our ASP (application service provider) model. Their link to PSI should be very reliable. They will order the computer equipment needed for their first term.
  • The DLS System IT committee - composed of heads of ITCs - met in De La Salle Araneta University (DLSAU) on January 23, 2003. The Committee had the opportunity to see the new System member. It has great potential for expansion and improvement. Like the Canlubang campus, DLSAU might need our assistance as well.
  • Planning Workshop - PSI had a planning workshop on January 30-31, 2003. The EVP defined a three-year strategic direction so that all PSI units can align their plans. I attended the said activity together with the Department Heads.
  • Documentation - I met with some key people from SMO regarding documentation of information systems development and maintenance. This has been one of the biggest challenges to SMO for many years. We are optimistic that this will have major improvements starting this term.
  • Performance Measures - Measuring our performance is one of the major paradigm shifts that we are facing. We need to do this together. We will all benefit from this because it will help us improve our outputs, our processes, and, most of all, ourselves. The measures have been defined and revisited already. It is up to us to make them part of our values and culture.
  • Server purchases -I met with some key people from CFO regarding the purchase of servers and storage. This is a major initiative towards radically improving our support infrastructure this school year. We hope to have enough funds to purchase branded servers for our Internet web pages, database, network management system, back-up, portal, and CFO installers. The next one will be done before the school year ends, again, provided we have sufficient funds.
  • Back up Faciltity - I am working on having an off-site back-up facility. The target location is DLSU-Dasmariñas. This will improve the reliability of our services. The back-up site will not only house our back-up media (e.g., tapes and CDROMs). It will also house a storage device for on-line back-up.
  • Human Metrics - We had a short “exercise” using the free service at www.humanmetrics.com. We have an interesting blend of personalities. Note that the results of that exercise depend on your answers, which could change anytime. Those who belong to the same “group” may not necessarily have the personality because the percentages vary (e.g., in terms of being introvert or extrovert). The value of this is that we are able to know and understand ourselves more. The results will be released as soon as everybody – including those on leave – have taken the test.
