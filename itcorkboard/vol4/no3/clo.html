Computer Laboratories Operations
 by: Glenn Bertulfo

What Lies Beyond 2003 ?

For starters, we see a new computer lab blossoming in Yuchengco Building. This is another state of the art facility which ITC hopes, could benefit not only CED people, but also other students as well.

The room will be designed like M306 (Phinma E-classroom) and will house the latest model of PC for multimedia applications -- branded Pentium 4 units with high-end multimedia paraphernalia. Not to mention the latest application software that will complement the hardware such as Macromedia and Adobe products which will surely entice all web-savvy students to try and experience a different kind of lab facility.

To date, room design as well as sourcing for the potential supplier of the mentioned hardware are underway. Target date of completion will be on or before May 15 - paving the way for a smooth transition towards the start of school year 2003-2004.

So, what lies beyond 2003 for CLO? Probably a whole lot more exciting and interesting developments that will help provide the university achieve its goal of becoming world-class. Just wait and see. The year 2003 promises to be one of the best years yet.
