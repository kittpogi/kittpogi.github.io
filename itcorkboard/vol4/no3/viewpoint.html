--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

These are just some of the feedbacks we have been getting regarding the redesigned DLSU-Manila website. Arguments fall under sticking to traditional and elegant designs, or keeping up with new technologies.

The new design is really in complete contrast to the one it preceded. While the old site was green and white, the new design is green and gray. While the old site had the Corinthian columns in all pages, the new design has it in selected links.

Though we have an urban atmosphere inside the campus, the people’s orientation still range from traditional to modern. Some like to keep things as simple as they are in some respects, while some like to keep up with changes at the pace they occur.

We have always prided ourselves with being the first in bringing our community the latest in the different fields we belong to, be it in the sciences, arts or technology. This is just one of the times when we did it again!

In the academic community, some were happy with the new appearance. It gives a sense of ‘high-tech look’ and somewhat pride for belonging in a school which employs state-of-the-art technology. However, the help desk received a lot of phone calls upon launching. Some computers did not have flash players, others have IE browser version older than required. The others were initially at a lost with the new navigational style.

Well, I guess our inadequacy is that we didn’t coordinate well with our Computer Facilities and Operations (CFO) Office to ensure that our viewers within the campus have the necessary software to appreciate our design. To cope with a new technology is a hassle but to find out that you’re not prepared for it is worse.

We knew that we would get varied reactions upon its launch as most people have been accustomed to seeing the old site for quite some time now. We just want to improve ourselves further so that we can render better service. We also aim to educate our public through our work by bringing them the best applicable technology we have now. We know that it won’t be fast and easy but we are confident that with the passing of time, they will all come to appreciate it.
