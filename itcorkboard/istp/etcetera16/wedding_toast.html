December 15, 2003
Wedding Toast
by Brian Co

Good afternoon everyone. Before anything else, I’d like to greet our newlyweds and their parents, Tito Vincent, Tita Pining, Tita Ping my warmest congratulations!

I first met Glen about five years ago. Soon after, he introduced me to Janita. Even then, it was apparent to me that they were truly in love with one another. It wasn’t because they were spouting “I Love Yous” every five minutes. But rather, it seemed to me that they drew strength, even sustenance from each other’s presence. Even if it was only watching movies or having a quiet dinner, that was enough for them.

I admire Janita for her directness, practicality, and level-headedness. She has a clear sense of where she wants to go and how to get there. Glen is certainly blessed to have found Janita. Likewise, Janita is lucky to have found Glen. He is well-liked because he is friendly, helpful, and ever-patient.

Undoubtedly, when two hearts are united in love, great things happen. And I am sure that we can expect great things from Glen and Jan.

I know that with God’s help, they will continue to achieve more successes. So, please raise your glass to Glen and Janita. May they always have good health, good fortune, plenty of chikitings, and last but not least, may they always be as in love with each other as they are at this moment.

Mabuhay!
