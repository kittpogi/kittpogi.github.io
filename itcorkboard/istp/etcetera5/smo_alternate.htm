    New SITE Coordinator (Alternate Version)
 

Even though he's out of season ("Ho, ho, ho!" Get it?), Brian Co still continues to hog (pun intended) the headlines. In an unprecedented move, he replaced current ISTC heartthrob Harold Bautista as the new coordinator for the SITE and ETP students under SMO. Mr. Co would ably fill in the space vacated by Mr. Bautista…and more! As Mr. Bautista puts it: "He (Mr. Co) has big shoes to fill, but with Brian, apparently my shoes aren't big enough." On his part, Mr. Co said: "It's an honor for me to follow the footsteps of my idol, Harold. I'll do my best to surpass the accomplishments of my hero."

Mr. Co has already met with the students, and he firmly put his foot down with regards to obeying office policies. The students, appalled by the tremors they felt, immediately agreed to abide by the rules set forth by Mr. Co. His term as coordinator would run for the entire duration of SY 2000-2001, unless he's overthrown in a coup d'état staged by the students. That remains to be seen. At present, Mr. Co could be seen literally throwing his weight around the students.

Brian's note:
Due to over-inhalation of toxic gases (e.g. carbon monoxide, garbage fumes, pesticides, his own body's "hotspots"), Harold has been suffering from constant hallucinations and over-indulgent wishful thinking. This article proves that he not only has staying power in terms of emitting "noxious" fumes (translation: malakas ang dating) but that he can also be very effective in spitting out over-imaginative yet humorous daydreams (translation: nakakatawa kahit na mabaho).

Harold Paulo D. Bautista 06/13/2000
