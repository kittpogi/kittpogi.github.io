Missive Mysteries & Mindbenders

A language as visual and familiar as English is ripe for distortion and ambiguity.

The figure on the left is an example of how Typography can be made to seem ambiguous.

If you read the center row across, you may see the middle character as a “B”.

If you read the center row from top to bottom, you would interpret the middle character as the number “13”.
