Mach’s Figure

This ambiguous illusion is an open book, literally. Are the pages fanned towards you or away from you?

If you see the figure is a book - as most of us do, you might assume there is text on the far-side because there is none on this side. This might cause you to interpret the visible side as the back of the book. But it can be seen either way. The vertical line can be the outside spine of the book, or it might suddenly change into the inside ‘gutter’ of the book.
