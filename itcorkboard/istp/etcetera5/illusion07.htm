3-in-1 Illusion

What you see at first, is a cube with a corner missing. Aspect One: Visualize the missing corner as a solid cube projecting out in front of the larger cube. Aspect Two: Next, make the larger cube turn into a “room” with vertical walls and floor. Aspect Three: And finally, watch as the missing corner feature appears to occupy the far corner of the “room”. It may take some trying, but once realized, you can make this illusion do your bidding. Want to build your own 3-D illusion cube? Watch the aspects change as you hold it!
