    What Do Girls Really Want From Men?
 
  1. Flowers: Proven by time, this lovely creation still manages to touch the soft spot of all girls.
  2. Being wooed from time to time (even if you're already going steady!): How much you would like to strangle your female partner if she continues on snobbing you after a lovers quarrel, don't stop doing this patience-provoking thing. She loves it!
  3. Being Pursued: They love to be pursued. I don't know why but if she's worth pursuing for, don't stop.
  4. Dine out: Many girls like to eat. But don't bring them into an eat-all-you-can restaurant. You'll regret it after some time.
  5. Dance/Party: Do I have to explain this? You can see dancing girls in TV, magazines, movies, cultural shows, ballet and inside a beerhouse.
  6. Surprises: Try a surprise visit, surprise date, surprise gift and any surprises that stimulate sudden excitement. But don't give her a surprise breakup. She will kill you!
  7. Simple Presents: It's not that girls are materialistic. Giving nice simple presents show her how much thoughtful you are. That's a plus "handsome point" for you!
  8. Shopping: Try accompanying her when she shops. It could be mind-deteriorating for us men (imagine standing with her without doing anything for hours) but it will pay off. Somehow.
  9. Love notes: This could sound corny to many but a little written message that says "I love you" or "I still care" can remove the devil in her.
  10. A Faithful Man: Even an unfaithful woman dreams of a faithful man. Who doesn't? We can have a whole new world if we could all be faithful to one another.

Contributed by: Johnny Perpetuo U. Benitez
