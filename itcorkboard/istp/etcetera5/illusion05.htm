Mystic Wheel

There are several interesting things happening in this illusion.

Notice a slight shimmer or flutter in the image? It always seems to happen whenever you are not looking. This proves your eye is round and can't focus all at once.

All the spokes are identical, evenly placed and flat, but there is a definite feeling of depth throughout. Some parts seem higher or lower than others.
