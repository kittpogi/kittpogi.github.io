Housekeeping Committee (5S) : Updates

Another year was over but not the Five S Committee's quest to promote the concept of Good Housekeeping. The committee coordinated with the Christmas Party Committee to include the Five S Jingle Contest in the Christmas Party Program. The fun continued when each office gave its best effort by participating in the jingle-making contest. The objectives of the project are to instill in the hearts and minds of everyone the essence of the Five S and to promote greater camaraderie among members of each unit. The winning jingle came from the very well prepared SMO. Their jingle entries were both in English and Filipino capturing both simplicity and world class interpretation of the Five S. On behalf of the committee we thank everyone for giving their support on the said project. As we hum to its tune let us blend in the lyrics. Here goes our jingle for Five S.
