A Farewell letter from Francis Mark D. Santos

Hi All,

It's official. This is my farewell to all of you whom I had a chance of working with.

After having worked here for some time, I have finally decided to change my Career and do something much more meaningful.

It is time to say a few thanks to my family and friends who always believed in me, and gave me the courage to take on the challenges that one faces during one's working life, and come out on top of them. I must thank you all for making me believe in my own determination. This gave me an opportunity to recognize the abilities I had that probably would not have come out hadn't it been for all of you.

I have decided now to take a break from work and am enrolling for a profession that will strengthen my skills and sharpen my motivational techniques. Above all, the profession promises to be more challenging and more exciting than anything I have ever undertaken before. Before I join the new profession, I am required to undergo intensive classroom and practical training, for which I have to go out of the country for a while. I know I'll miss you, but at the end you'll all be proud of me.

I am sure I have found the right career for myself after all these years. I stand wiser today, and I am sure it is for the better.

I am attaching a prospectus of the course I am about to undertake and request you to take a look. I am sure that quite a few of you may also like to take a break and develop these new skills.

If you are interested for a change in career, please see the prospectus attached. It might change your life.

I'll never forget you guys..... Thank you very much...

Just me,

Kitt Pogi :)

PS. This is also the reason why I'm not having a hair cut for quite some time now...
