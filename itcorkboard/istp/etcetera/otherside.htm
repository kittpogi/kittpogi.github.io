The Other Side of ISTC

The ISTP, as the name connotes, are sometimes branded as "the techies" and "the systems group". Though we don’t deny that the connotation is not accurate, we still would like to be known as normal as the rest of the people in the campus.

As a way of building the camaraderie within the office, we have scheduled a once-a-month unwinding session. A specific or combination of office units, and birthday celebrants for the month will host the sessions. The session will include film-viewing, videoke, and of course...EATING!

However, to balance the cholesterol build-up in our bodies, sports groups are being organized. A bowling session has started last month. Schedule for table tennis games is in the works. Volleyball sessions will be scheduled this summer to prepare early for the next school year's Faculty Sportsfest (to vie for a grand slam victory!).   We also hope to revive the ballroom dancing sessions that we have started last December 1998 in the ISTC Christmas Party, where we found out that a lot of people can dance gracefully. Recently, we have also discovered that some ISTPs know how to sing and to play musical instruments. A move to form a choir is being planned.

So you see, we’re not only "techies" but multi-talented individuals. As our mission-vision states, the ISTP shall seek to be a highly effective team of committed and motivated Christian Achievers spearheading not only the application of information technology but also the development of holistic human resources.

Celine V. Rivera 02/19/1999
