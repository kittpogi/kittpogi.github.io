    Health Stuff
 

Here are some tips from a Nutrition Seminar

1. The proportion of the waist to hips should be:

.92 for males
.75 for females

* To compute: size of waist divided by size of hips; anything higher than the recommended number is considered fat or pear-shape. Anything lower would mean the person is thin or under nourished.

2. Fibre is needed by the body to flush out toxic materials that adhere to the colon and intestines.

3. How to keep slim:

- avoid snacking
- less fat intake
- eat in smaller proportions
- no desserts

* A slice of cake is equivalent to 2 servings of rice

4. Diet and exercise is a successful combination

5. Avoid the intake of diet pills. These have adverse side effects.

6. Senescence -

- process of growing old
- another term for ageing
- is a progressive physical and mental debilitation caused by the generation of    free radicals
- free radicals come from food, even in our surroundings (like office walls)
- if we can retard these free radicals, we can retard the ageing process.

7. 10 Basic pills to help keep you young

- multivitamins
- Vitamin E (100 -400 IU)
- Vitamin C (500-1500mg)
- Beta carotene (10-15 mg)
- Chromium
- Calcium
- Zinc
- Selenium
- Magnesium
- Coenzyme Q-10

8. Antiageing Diet Strategy

- eat fruits and vegetables
- eat fish
- eat soybean foods
- drink tea (dark teas will do but herbal and green teas are better)
- drink a maximum of 3 cups a day
- avoid fat
- go easy on meats
- cut sweets
- eat garlic
- consider the pros and cons of alcohol (alcohol in the right amount promotes the circulation of the blood)

contributed by Celine V. Rivera 02/19/1999
