    Spot the Mistakes...
 

Social gatherings are an important part of business life. They're a place to relax with old friends, meet new prospects, search out and seal deals.

They're also a deadly trap. Relax too, much, be too casual, break society's "hidden" rules and you can easily sabotage yourself. And you may never know why!

In our cartoon there are 12 "sins" being committed. Take a good look. Resolve to avoid them like the plague. Let your unwary colleagues step on these social landmines if they must. Just make sure you stay clear, out of trouble and on the rise... to the top!
