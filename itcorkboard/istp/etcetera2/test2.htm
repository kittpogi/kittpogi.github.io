    Man or Woman?

 

Is it possible for a man from a woman by their reactions to everyday situations? It is - for a psychologist. But how about you? Test your knowledge of men-women differences on these questions based on studies.

  1. Two persons dip into a box of chocolates. One eats the entire piece at once; the other bites it to see what is inside. Who's the biter? Man or Woman?
  2. At a party, a guest said that most people don't want to visit the moon. One listener agrees, citing opinion surveys. Another disagrees, asserting: "I don't think that's true. I want to go." Who judges other people's reactions this way? Man or Woman?
  3. A husband and wife are in the living room reading, while their sick baby is asleep upstairs. One of them looks up and says:"You're worrying about the baby, aren't you? The doctor says he'll be fine." Who was the first to perceive the other's concern? Man or Woman?
  4. A man and his wife are dining in a restaurant. One orders unusual dishes the family has never tried before. The other sticks to more familiar fare. Who's the food adventurer? Man or Woman?
  5. The same couple enter a shop. On one side is an array of new products; on the other is a display of familiar merchandise. Which one is fascinated by the newer items? Man or Woman?
  6. Two shoppers goes into a store for new clothes. One asks for a particular garment and color. But who just asks vaguely to see some clothes? Man or Woman?
  7. The car's gas gauge is dangerously low. One person in the car wants to stop right away and fill up. The other wants to make it across the bridge to where the gas is cheaper. Which is the chance-taker? Man or Woman?
  8. En route to a strange destination, a couple realize that they're lost. One wants to ask directions, the other to cruise around looking. Who's the cruiser? Man or Woman?
  9. The gas-station attendant asks, "Check your oil and water?" This motorist says: "Not now, I'm in hurry." The driver's spouse, however, waits for the checkup. Who won't wait? Man or Woman?
  10. Three cars are stopped abreast for a red light. As the signal turns green, one tries to beat the others to a getaway. Who's the drag-racer? Man or Woman? (Bonus Question)
  11. An especially sad movie comes to the neighborhood. Who will feel sadder as the tearful scenes unreel? Men or Women?
