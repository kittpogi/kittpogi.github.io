    Outings...
 

Last summer, ISTC had some fun in the sun, sand and sea by having outings. Take note - outings! That means we didn't have just one but actually two outings (well, three if you count our trip to Iloilo, Bacolod and Guimaras.)

Here are some pictures from the said outings....

 

Puerto Galera, Mindoro Occidental
(April 8-10, 1999)

The land of the endangered "tamaraws", and we're glad we didn't meet one in the beach. ISTC have been here a couple of times already that is why some people opted not to come. It's definitely more fun if more people joined in.

Feeling lonely? Sleeping in the beach and watching "shooting stars" pass by should do you good! Ingat lang at baka ka masipon!

Fontana Resort, Pampanga
(June 19-20, 1999)

Not so summer outing right inside the heart of the former US Air Base. This resort is a great one, complete with "first class" amenities (You get to stay in a European villa! - complete except the stove, to the point of cooking breakfast out of town!) and a tsunami pool. In town, you get to eat a RUMBA meal ala Friday's servings yet affordable enough. Whew, can't wait to come back here - sans shopping of course!

Iloilo, Bacolod, Guimaras
(May 21- 25, 1999)

Welcome to the land of La Paz Batchoy, Crab "Kasag" special, Chicken Inasal, Piyaya, Sunday gimik-sunrise blues, Waterfalls and Hiking, Church-hopping, Sea Angel's Ferry navigating, and Beaches where hens lay "golden" eggs costing P10.00 each. So overwhelming was the language barrier that, we (Tagalogs), were involuntarily reduced to a bunch of clueless, pathetic guys! 

Andam! Pagbalik namon, kabalo na kami mag-Ilonggo! Mabawi guid kami!

There you have it! Next year's outing will definitely be more fun since preparations are already set (Palawan ba o Boracay?). Nag-iipon na para mapuntahan ang mga hindi pa napupuntahan.

Francis Mark D. Santos, Maria Stephanie G. Gonzaga 07/06/1999
