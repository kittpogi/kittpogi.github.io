What makes a great kisser?

Men are often too rough, aggressive, fast-paced, and lack knowledge of the four P's of kissing: patience, passion, pacing, and pressure. In Mirror magazine's special "Kiss" issue this week, here are some of the pointers offered:

First focus on the lips. Don't reach right away for other sensitive parts like the neck and ears so as not to divert attention from the kiss.

Move slowly. Jumping too quickly to other activities and engaging in excessive teasing can become annoying.

Explore some often overlooked areas like the corners of the mouth, temples, eyelids, fingertips, and palms of the hand.

Check your breath.

Deliver your kisses with feeling, passion, and care. Learn to change pace and alternate pressure and location.

Practice, practice, practice.

Manila Bulletin, February 13, 2000
