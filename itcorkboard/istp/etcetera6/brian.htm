    Brian's Delights
 

The first one to answer both questions correctly wins a FREE MERIENDA at Z2 c/o BOC.  Deadline is on Monday at 3 pm.

  1. You are lost in a forest. The forest is between two cities. To the West is Erap City, the inhabitants of which always lie. To the East is Paradise City. People in Paradise City always tell the truth. You want to go to Paradise City.  Then you see a man who can be from Erap City or Paradise City. You can ask him only one question. Which question will you ask him to know for sure where Paradise City is?
  2. Two strangers from different parts of the Philippines both build similar apartment buildings in their home towns. By chance, they both forget an important part of their project. They each, once again by chance, call the same hardware store and order the missing items. The prices they are quoted are as follows: 

    one will cost them 200 pesos.
    two will cost them 200 pesos.
    twelve will cost them 400 pesos.
    and a hundred and forty-four will cost 600 pesos.

    What was the item they needed?
