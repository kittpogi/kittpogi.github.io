    Lighter side of the Abu Sayaf Saga
 

It was strongly recommended that Erap start hiring Muslims and commission them to head up the government agencies.  

Initially, with their qualifications and experiences, the following will be assigned to the these agencies of the government:

  • Dept. of Justice - Abu Gado
  • Dept. of National Defense - Abu Rido
  • Dept. of Finance - Abu Nado
  • Dept. of Social Welfare - Abu Loy
  • Bureau of Customs - Abu Tan
  • Phil. National Police - Abu Sado
  • Dept. of Tourism - Abu So
  • Dept. of Health - Abu Lario
  • Dept. of Agriculture - Abu Kado
