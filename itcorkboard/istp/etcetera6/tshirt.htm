    The ITC T-shirt
 

During our first term ITC Staff Meeting, the concept of having an ISTC/ITC souvenir shirt came about through the Team Building Committee.  As suggested, expenses will be covered by the prize money the ITC received for last school year’s performance.  

At dahil nga nagpalit na tayo ng pangalan, mula ISTC ay naging ITC na.  Naisipang magpagawa ng isang souvenir T-shirt para may konting senti.  

So for you ITPs waiting for the ISTC/ITC souvenir t-shirt, the wait could finally be over.  The Team Building Committee has already chosen a supplier.  We’re just trying to make sure that the final product will be good and that it will fit (kasi nga hindi naman pwedeng free size ang kunin natin, lam nyo na! ).  Abangan na lang po ninyo ang announcement para sa mga sizes.   

Well, about the ITC Polo shirt. Initially, the design had the campus name.  Just this week, however, SPM mentioned that the campus name should be dropped.  Kasi medyo may kamahalan talaga pag may campus name.  Anyways, we’ll be asking for your sizes soon ok.  (Click image for a larger view)

To LEM (& I think yung bestfriend nya dun na si Kitt) at sa mga nagbigay ng input, salamat sa maarte n’yong design!!!!  


 

 

 

Joel G. Kisteria, 10/18/00
