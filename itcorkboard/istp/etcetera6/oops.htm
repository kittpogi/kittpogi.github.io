    The Story behind Ooops!

 

The Ooops! logbook was finally launched last September 05, 2000 through e-mail by yours truly. 

The idea of Ooops! was conceived after a series of very funny situations occurred. We planned to have a record book or something where we can write/record the things that happen in the office that made us laugh and laugh and laugh out loud and made us teary from laughing hard...but it was all just a thought then… 

What really prompted me to come up with the logbook was the “Wall-Clock Ooops!”  whose main character was Johnny B.  Then it was followed by another big event which we may call as “Wall-Clock 2” also starred by Johnny B.  So we have Johnny to thank for, for the birth of Ooops! (Thank you Johnny! *clap! clap!* ) If you’re curious about the story of wall clock 1 & 2 just read the story from our very own Ooops! logbook.   We are still waiting for Wall-Clock 3! I hear that it’s still in the making. 

We (Lorna & I ) originally named the logbook as “Ek-ek!”.  A few other choices were “Kaek-ekan!’ and “ITC Ek-ek!”…then suddenly Ooops! just popped up! So finally, we now have a record book nicknamed "Ooops!" where we can log every single funny thing. (huh?!)

O di ba? we'll be able to look back to those happy moments by sweeping through the "Ooops!" logbook! Yehey! :)  Maybe someday we’ll give prizes to those who have the most number of contributions. (wishful thinking lang po iyon).

Special thanks to Ate Sally for the Record book (sa kanya galing yon.), to BigBoyBrian for wrapping the logbook, to Lorna for helping me realize this idea, and to mommy Trish for sharing that they have this in her previous job. 

I’m encouraging all of you to log your funny experiences in Ooops! Share those funny moments with everyone and make everyone happy! And probably learn from them, too…

 

Love Leah Malig 10/13/00
