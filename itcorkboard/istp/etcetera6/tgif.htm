    Fridays @ ITC
 

As agreed upon during the ITC Staff Meeting last term, the Friday Night Activities (which were held every Friday of the month) was now reduced to two Fridays of each month.  What remains the same, though, is that ITC units still take turn in sponsoring the activity. 

…..the SMO group went on with a classic movie to kickoff the first sponsored Friday for August.  “My Fair Lady”, starring Donita Rose look-a-like Audrey Hepburn.  For those ITPs who saw it, aba’y pagbabalik tanaw yon ah!

…..their next Friday was an early evening-noontime show (gets n'yo?).  The success of their “PERA o BAYONG” was perhaps, as some analysts would say, brought by the performance of the famed “Khendeng Girls & Boys”.  If you were not there to see it, oh-mi-gosh! you missed the rarest spectacle to ever happen, even rarer than appearance of Haley’s Comet.  Ano ‘kamo yon… ano pa edi nag sayaw si Brian! (ang kuya ni Raymond-Glen). PAMBIHIRA!!!  And to Ms Celine, congratulations for winning the bayong…este pera pala.

…..the –ber months started with a “Sing-and-Win” program.  The first Friday Activity by PRIO for September was attended by the wannabes.  I wanna be the Mega star, I wanna be Karen C., I wanna be Jaya, I wanna be Frank or Matt, at marami pang iba.  Although the Gong master heard all these wannabes, there was still one group to which the gong was not banged... for that they won!  The concert was continued by the Gong master and more wannabes later that evening!  Hanep pala si Miss Daisy mag-concert, may khendeng na, may audience participation pa!

…..and because we (most of us) like simple betting games, the last Friday of September was capped by PRIO’s GOBINGO event.  The same group that joined in the “Sing-and Win”, was welcomed in the GOBINGO.  ITPs went to the extreme in thinking of the right answers to the GOBINGO Masters’ questions.  Although most of the questions need not be thought about naman pala.  Pero grabe si Miss Sexy Ampel (member ng Khendeng Girls) sa pag-alala ng mga celebs & hitshows ng 70s & 80s…ang husay pala natin sa mga tanong na JOLOGS!!!  (tip: tandaan na ninyo yung mga trivial stuff dito sa office, nicknames, location/cubicle, favorites….teka slumbook na yata iyon ah!  It might come in handy, the next time around).

KEEP SUPPORTING!!!

& for the other units….sana’y mabusog nyo kami!!!

 

 

Joel G. Kisteria, 10/17/00
