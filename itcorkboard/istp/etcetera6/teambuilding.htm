   Anything Goes

 

The newest ITC Team Building Activity, Anything Goes, will already begin on October 16, 2000.  4 groups within ITC will compete every week to earn the most points by the time the ITC Christmas party will be held.  The team with the most points will, of course, win a prize!

Anything Goes was initially implemented within SMO by Brian Co.  Because some staff would spend time during lunch breaks and after office hours figuring out what the puzzles meant, ITC Team Building Committee (TBC) Chairperson Amy Salcedo proposed that this be implemented office-wide via the TBC.  Initial implementation comprised basically of posting copies of the puzzles and distributing these to the CFO and DAO/DOCU bulletin boards. 

After enough of a compilation of puzzles was set up (thanks to Brian Co, Lorna Acorda and Loveleah Malig), the activity was finally launched during the October 3 ITC Staff Meeting with a game (thanks to Loveleah Malig).   The mechanics of the activity are simple:  (1) a new set of 6 puzzles will be posted every 2 weeks; (2) the teams should submit their answers (ASAP) by e-mail to Vivian Sy, and the first team to answer all 6 puzzles correctly will earn a point; (3) the points earned by each group will be e-mailed to all the ITC staff before a new puzzle is posted; and (4) the team with the most points will be declared winner during the ITC Christmas party.
