    5S
 

The way to mobilize things around the office is to start giving the Five S a serious thought, don’t you think so? The Five S committee together with the help of the Coordinators made things possible last Aug. 11, 2000 on a Friday afternoon affair. Almost everyone made it a point to participate in the clean up hour! Oh boy! What a relief to see everyone’s work area clean and spacious huh!!! How much junk of floopy disks and waste scratch papers did everyone pile up in their trash!?! How many Lorns, Glen, Noel?

Hey, did everybody use the standard labels we have provided? If not, then you still have ample time to do so, another check-up will be done soon.

More tips on how to maintain the Five S will be e-mailed to you. We will give the Five S its re-launching and it will start with a Big BANG soon! Cis has already prepared the eye-catching logo that will surely catch your stare. So ‘til the next update…

Ooops, wait… before I go, I would like to remind you to be aware of the unannounced audit!! ! I could smell it. It is almost around the corner… like dust in the wind and like Christmas time ho! ho! ho!

 

Eileen D. Manabat, 10/18/00
