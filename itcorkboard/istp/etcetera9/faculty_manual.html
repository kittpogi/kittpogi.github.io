Faculty Manual Proposals

As new Faculty Manual representatives for ITC, Sammy Malllare, Brian Co, Nick Bonao and Catalina Quioge, they have discussed some matters regarding the Faculty Manual Proposals, They are:

1. Hiring procedures

Adoption of written standardized skills exam

2. Retirement

a. Remove age requirement
b. Allow for a minimum of 20 years of service

3. Longevity Benefits

Revise longevity pay entitlement for ITPs with the appropriate number of years of service:

a. 10 to less than 15 years - one-month basic salary

b. 15 to less than 20 years - two-month basic salary

c. 20 to less than 25 years - three-month basic salary

d. 25 to less than 30 years - four-month basic salary

e. 30 to less than 35 years - five-month basic salary

f. over 35 years - six-month basic salary

4. Maternity Leave

"First two children…" should read as "First two deliveries…"

5. Vacation Leave

a. Clarify pro-rated computations if the official hiring date is not June

b. Conversion of 10 Vacation Leave days to cash

c. Option is to accumulate unused VLs

6. Sick Leave

Clarify pro-rated computations if the official hiring date is not June

7. Service Leave

a. Increase to 25 working days but with the same service component

b. Five days leave without pay should not disrupt continuity of service

8. Study and/or Research Leave

Clarify sections 5.3.1.2 and 5.3.1.3

9. Study and/or Research Work for Personal Reasons

Clarify difference with Business or Practice of Profession Leave
Note: The main difference is that Business or Practice of Profession Leave allows one to secure employment during the leave period.

10. Business or Practice of Profession Leave

Clarify the number of times it can be availed of Note: Currently, there is no limit to the number of times it can be availed provided that the supervisor approves the applicant's request.

11. Military Service or Secondment Leave

Clarify if with pay

12. PSI Education/Welfare Fund (Brother President Scholarship Program)

If the ITP's child did not pass in any of the DLSU system schools, DLSU should also shoulder the tuition
(or fraction thereof) if the child enrolls in other schools

13. Disability Benefits

Change from "…not less than 15,000 to no more than 150,000…" to "…not less than 15,000 to no more than 300,000…"

14. Death benefits

Clarify computation of contributions

15. CEAP membership

a. Clarify membership requirements

b. Clarify status of plans B and D

16. Faculty Development Incentive

(Implementing Guidelines: Section 10)
For 6th and 7th year - 50% of basic salary

17. Faculty Development Incentive regarding graduate studies:

a. ITP can avail even if he/she is less than a year in service
Clarify distinction if enrolled within and outside DLSU

b. ITP can take a course not related to work

-An ad hoc committee will determine if a course/degree is related to work or not
-The composition of the committee will be the same as that for the ITP's promotion board
-Make a list of graduate degrees that are related/applicable to existing job positions

18. For hiring/promotion/reclassification/permanency of director, board composition will be:

a. EVPs (Manila and PSI)

b. At least two ITC Coordinators

c. ITP Representative

19. Evaluation

a. 3.75 instead of "better than satisfactory" or "above-average"

b. Justify promotion/reclassification if ITP evaluation is lower than 3.75

20. Paternity Leave

Increase to ten working days

21. Eye care allowance

a. P 2,000 a year

b. Cumulative

c. Non-taxable

22. Honorarium

Inclusion in 13th month pay

23. Meal Allowance

a. P500 for every 4 hours

b. Excluding Director and Coordinators

c. Subject to "Arranged Schedule" policies

24. Tax Shield

P 4,000, Subject to the condition that base pay computation includes the tax shield

25. Birthday Leave

One day on your birthday or if it falls on a weekend or holiday, anytime within the week.

We will keep you updated on other concerns and topics that may arise from other meetings.
