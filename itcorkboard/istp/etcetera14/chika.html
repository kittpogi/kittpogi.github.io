ITChik-a
by: Chief Bubwit and the Rectal Thermometers

It’s good to be back!

There were no yellow ribbons, but as I reached the fourth flight of stairs going to G309, a smile drew on my lips, and then one by one, some familiar faces welcomed me… well it feels good to be back. I missed those friendly and familiar faces. I missed the La Casita dishes. I missed my keyboard, and oops… I think my PC wallpaper needs some facelift.

It was just a six-month leave, and generally everything stayed the way they were when I left last December. I was taken aback though with the “welcome picture” posted on my good old desk mirror. It was from my DOCU officemates who always find a fitting place for the champion pose of ITC’s undying matinee idol, Pep. Well, that picture never fails to serve its purpose whatever it is aimed to accomplish.

Anyway, for the second time here in ITC, I felt like a newbie. I was even having a hard time unearthing the files and folders which before, I could access even with my eyes closed. On my first day of work, I spent my first few hours running over my couple of thousands emails, and oh well, nearly a couple of thousands of them are junk.

And of course, I spent sometime catching up on the latest ITChika! At syempre, may scoop ako para sa inyong lahat na galing pa sa aking umuusok na mailbox. Isang sulat na mula sa isa nating bubwit. Ating basahin ang kanyang ilalahad...

Dear Chief Bubwit,

Itago na lang natin si lalake sa pangalang "JoeGie". Nagkayayaan mag-inuman isang gabi sina JoeGie at ang tatlo nyang mga kaopisina. Nagpunta sila sa bahay ni "Mr. Blue" (hindi sad, mahilig lang sa kulay blue).

Since kagagaling lang sa basketball, 'di pa kumakain ang mga nagpunta sa bahay, at si Mr. Blue ay nag-prisintang bumili ng pagkain. Si JoeGie naman ay nagpupumilit na sasama daw at dadaan sa bahay nila para kumuha ng mga bala ng videoke.

So umalis yung dalawa at may dalang lechon manok pagbalik. Kumain ng dinner yung apat, at pagkatapos ay nag-inuman habang nanonood ng Maalaala Mo Kaya (life story ni Mahal) at paminsan-minsan ay kay "tita swarding's".

Tila may naaalala si JoeGie sa salitang "mahal" (buti na lng 'di swarding J)
kaya pinaandar na niya yung mga dala n'yang VCD (The best of Jose Mari Chan, The best of Ogie Alcasid) pagkatapos ng "Maalaala". Nakapuna yung tatlo nyang kasama (alt+F3) na parang "feel na feel" niya yung mga love songs na kinakanta niya kaya sinimulan nila ang pag-interrogate sa suspect.

Alam naman nung tatlong kasama ni JoeGie na this guy's in love with his "pare." At nung tanungin nila kung may ginawa na syang "nice moves" sa kanyang pare, ang sagot lang niya ay: "Hindi pwede."

Buong gabi, kahit anong itanong nung tatlo n'yang kasama, ang laging sinasagot lang ni JoeGie ay... you guessed it: "Hindi pwede." Ayaw naman n'yang ipaliwanag kung ano ang ibig sabihin ng "hindi pwede". All the while, lalong nagkakaroon ng feelings ang kanyang pagsabay sa mga awit sa dala nyang VCDs.

Since walang nakuhang matinong impormasyon yung tatlong kasama ni JoeGie, naisipan nila na magyayaya ulit sila ng isa pang inuman, pero yung pare naman ni Joe-Mari ang iimbitahin nila, minus JoeGie.

Abangan...
