Remembering our Spirits

"And do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind, that you may prove what the will of God is, that which is good and acceptable and perfect." (NAS) Romans 12:2

The ISTC Team Building Committee decided to organize a recollection for the whole of ISTC, the goal is for the ISTPs to realize and radiate their spirituality into the workplace. One whole day was set aside from work to lift up and remember the ISTP spirits through the guidance of Mr. Vic Abrenica, fondly called as "Manong Vic". It took place at the Agno House last mm/dd/yyyy from 8am to 5pm.

The recollection kicked off with a workshop where the ISTPs were grouped into five and each one was asked to draw his own interpretation or symbolism of God. This activity showcased the ISTPs variant and meaningful perspectives of who God is in their lives. And being an Almighty God that He is, surely He is able to capture and fulfill everyone's expectation of Him and much more.

A game that lifted team spirit followed where everyone energetically lined themselves up according to the criteria given by the game master. The hearty lunch that followed satisfied the gastronomes of ISTC and packed each one with energy for the afternoon activities.

Another group activity entitled "Bartimaeus on the Road," made each ISTP realize where he stands along the road of discipleship. Each ISTP had to answer 10 questions regarding how other people influence their lives and on the other end, how their actions affect other people. After everybody truthfully answered each question, members of the group shared their responses.

Manong Vic later on gave the ISTC group an insight on the "Job Traps" that the ISTPs might fall into, or already have been confined into, how to get out of it, and more importantly how to watch out for it. An inspiring sharing of Manong Vic's discipleship and love life has transpired before the group decided to call it a day.

Much as we feed our stomach, it is also vital to feed our soul and put into focus our relationship with God and how we can uphold this relationship into our daily lives. Whether the ISTC Team Building Committee was successful in fulfilling its goal thru this recollection, only each one's heart and soul could answer.

The fear of the LORD leads to life: Then one rests content, untouched by trouble. (NIV) Proverbs 19:23

Joie B. Alvarez 06/23/2000
