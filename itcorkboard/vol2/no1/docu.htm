    DOCU Office Updates

 

AUN Conference

The Docu Office took part in the past ASEAN University Network (AUN) Conference which was held last March 27-29, 2000 by designing its website and facilitating registration and reservation through the use of online forms.  The conference aims to enhance regional identity and solidarity, upgrade manpower skills and capabilities, and hasten the dissemination of information, among IT institutions of AUN member universities.

CLO Wallpaper and Flyers

The CLO has decided to change its official computer lab wallpaper through the assistance of the Docu office. The new wallpaper has a solid Lasallian green background with spherical scribble highlights. It also displays the new logo of the Information Technology Center. The Computer Laboratory Policies are clearly enumerated on the right side of the wallpaper to continuously remind the students of the do’s and don’ts while using the computer labs. To fully support its clients, CLO again asked the creative assistance of the Docu office to design flyers which will serve as a guide to freshmen students in availing and using the University’s computer facilities, specifically the computer labs. The vibrantly designed flyer basically contains the procedures in activating internet account, lab assignments, and contact information. These flyers were distributed….

Online… MBA Online!

The splash page of the MBA Online can now be visited at http://www.dlsu.edu.ph/mbaonline. The site has a simple but classy layout.  Unfortunately, only the first page is available for viewing, the Docu office still awaits for the Marketing Communication (MCO) to provide the input file to finally complete the web site.

ISTC Handbook and JDQS on the Web

The ISTC Handbook and Job Description & Qualification Standards were at last finalized and printed. To us ITPs, these manuals mean two basic things: what is expected of us by the University, and what we could expect from the University in return.

The manuals can be borrowed at the Docu Office, but to keep it readily available to everyone (ITPs only), the manuals can be accessed on the intranet provided you have an Acrobat Reader installed in your PC, the URL is http://intranet.dlsu.edu.ph/offices/istc and click on the ISTC JDQS link.

Joie B. Alvarez 06/02/2000
