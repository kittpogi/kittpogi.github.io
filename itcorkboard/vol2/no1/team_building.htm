    Team Building's Friday Night Activities
 

The ITC Team Building Committee initiated a…. watchamacallit - "TGIF"… just this 3rd Term of SY 1999-2000, where each office will have a chance to sponsor a gimik/activity every Friday night for one whole month.

Ideally, the TGIF thing is a way of saying "Come on guys! Let's get together before the weekend." That's because after a week of working, we need to find time to relax. Besides, we always put the icing on top of the cake, right?

To give you a run down of what went on (and to give the offices a good idea of what they can come up with next time)…….here's history:

SMO dudes & dudettes (for February) went on show first with the following production:

  • The Bone Collector (movie) - 04 Feb
  • Good Will Hunting (movie) - 11 Feb
  • The Green Mile (movie) - 25 Feb

    These are good movies & they had no ticket prices, so how could you've missed it.

PRIO busy buddies & a bee (for March) came next:

  • Breakfast of Champions (movie) - 03 Mar
  • Bingo PRIO (non-franchise of BINGO Filipino) - 10 Mar
  • Dance Revo (for party animals) - 17 Mar
  • Videoke Night (sing-a-long) - 31 Mar

    New talents were discovered. (huh? =) )

DAO/DO was supposed to host the April extravaganzas but, due to the unavailability of most of the foreign artists they invited to perform, no activity was held. They vowed, though, to make it up next time. (Not much was missed in April since ISTC went to Pagudpod, Ilocos Norte for the summer outing)

As of this writing, CFO/CLO (for June) group is the host.

  • Sing-Galing (sing-a-long) - 12 June

    A grand last TGIF activity of the group for this month.

TGIF will still be continued this SY 2000-2001. So the offices could prepare in advance, here is the schedule:

  • DAO/DO - July
  • SMO - August
  • PRIO/OD - September
  • CFO/CLO - October

Kaya kung ordinaryo na ang day to day mo, nakakabato at nakakaloko. 'wag mong gawing ordinaryo ang Biyernes mo. Gawing light & bubbly ang Biyernes….so, ano? Join na! Tara let's….

The TGIF event is just one of the many activities the Team Building Committee has organized for the ITC. Whole year round, there are lots more in store!

Joel G. Kisteria/Team Building Committee 06/29/2000
