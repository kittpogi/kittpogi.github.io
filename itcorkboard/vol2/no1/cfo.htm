    CFO Updates
 

CFO's Much Awaited Facelift Now a Reality

Last June 17, CFO's staff area had a major surgical operation that lasted for two days. A modular setup was put into place separating the PC Network Engineer Group, the Systems Group and the Helpdesk Group, giving the office a more professional look.

The main reason behind this reorientation is simply to provide a more convenient working environment for all CFO staff. Aside from increasing the allowable number of staff to 15 (from 13), the new design actually allowed for the maximization of the office area-a separate conference area is now a reality especially if the office conference room is fully booked.

Fifty Phone Lines Added in the Dial-up List

In line with the office vision of being the most reliable service unit in the campus, CFO recently acquired 50 additional telephone lines to boost its internet providing capacity.

With this development, it is hoped that more and more students and faculty members will have the opportunity to enjoy the internet in the convenience of their homes.

Optimum Does It Again

In lieu of the recent Preventive Maintenance Contractor bidding, the office congratulates the Optimum Group (OTRC) for winning over their bidding mates a few months ago.

So to Rodel, Randy, Aqui and Rino, keep up the good work! We hope to get the most of you for the rest of the year (hehehe).

Glen D. Bertulfo 07/10/2000
