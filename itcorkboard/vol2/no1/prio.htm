Busy Buddies Corner

Hello! Fellow ISTPs,

Another school year has just ended, things and people come and go, but truly the memories linger on. So let’s move on.

What materialized last January to May with the busy buddies? Apart from the new staff we had, read on the list:

  1. The Disbursement Review is now at the EVP’s office for approval. (Let’s wait for the verdict)

  2. The ATM Fund Transfer Study, which aims to decongest students who pay their tuition fees at the Accounting Office, was launched. (Let’s see if students will grab the benefit we just have for them)

  3. Adjustment Review had its pilot run these last two days of May. (Success!?! Let’s wait for the upcoming meeting for the comments)

  4. The Pre-enrollment Review momentarily is just waiting for the program of Sir James before it could be finalized and be given for approval. (Can’t wait to see the smile of the students once this is tested for pilot run!)

  5. We have another round of IQA for better understanding which was again facilitated by Amy. (People are concerned what is Clause 4.9? Kailangan ba ng pandesal at cappuccino para ma-memorize na natin ng husto!?!)

  6. We also had our workshop and the outcome was that we had our Business Process and it was finally approved by SPM.

  7. The Good Housekeeping Committee wherein our Mommy Trish is actively participating had just finished the 5S audit. (hmmm… best model cubicle definitely not me, though I know who might just win, I really wish I could make my table look like his (who is he? I bet you all know him), so clean, free and organized)

  8. The Teambuilding Committee headed by Amy had another successful outreach recently (see related article) for the children of Hospicio De San Jose. (Isa lang ang masasabi ko masarap talaga yung spaghetti ni Joel. Sabihin ninyong hindi at nang sumakit ang tiyan ng lahat ng kumain 'di ba!?!

  9. The Summer Outing Committee where in Mommy Trish and our one and only gentleman in PRIO (ehem…ehem agree ka ba Bossing!?!), Joel had their share of headaches during the preparations and of course million times of fun in Pagudpud… "napudpod ang p'wit nila sa biyahe" (Sa lahat ng sumama at may dalang pasalubong maraming salamat!)

  10. The Excellence Committee where in yours truly is a member had just completed the tallying for the Best Idea and Excellence Award. (Content na lang ng mga plaque tapos na. Sige kahit hindi namin sabihin alam ko na manlilibre naman ang mananalo!)

  11. Oops… one more thing, our office made another exciting participation during the sponsored mass and the Friday activity sponsorship for the month of March. Remember Bingo at Kapihan? (Sa bagong kasal, nakatulong sana kahit papaano yung napanalunan mo sa Bingo! Want some round again Joie and Mama? Siguro sa susunod kayo na ang mananalo. Sa mga nag-Dance Revo, sino nga ba ang Dance Revo king? 'Di ba si mahiyaing Ms. Vivs ang Dance Revo Queen? Mga katoto noong gabi sa pagvi-videoke, salamat sa pagshi-share ninyo ng talent, atin-atin na lang ang mga talent na 'yan hehehehe)

Bitin ba kayo? Hanggang dyan lang muna ha! Sa susunod ulit.

Eileen D. Manabat 06/01/00
