ISTC to ITC

Since it was decided by the IT System Committee to carry the same name  for all the IT units of the System Schools, ISTC is now called Information Technology Center or ITC. This will be the fifth time the Center has changed its name over its 30 years of existence. As a result, the newly designed ISTC logo has to be changed, and the new one is applicable for all the IT Center of the System Schools.  The elements used in the new logo was adopted from the old ISTC logo, hence the same symbolism, the same servitude, under a new name. Friday Night Activities

The ITC Team Building Committee initiated a…. watchamacallit - "TGIF"… just this 3rd Term of SY 1999-2000, where each office will have a chance to sponsor a gimik/activity every Friday night for one whole month.

Ideally, the TGIF thing is a way of saying "Come on guys! Let's get together before the weekend." That's because after a week of working, we need to find time to relax. Besides, we always put the icing on top of the cake, right? [more on these]
