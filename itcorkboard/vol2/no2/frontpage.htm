ITC and Globe Telecom offer Infotext Services to Students


During the last Undergraduate Adjustment Period (September 11-12, 2000), the ITC in cooperation with Globe Telecom tested out a new information service which utilizes Short Message Service (SMS),popularly known as text messaging.  The new system, named DLSU Infotext, allows students equipped with a Globe Telecom cellular phone to check the availability and schedule of a subject that they wish to enroll in. 

To do so, the student has to send the text message “DLSU SUBJ BUSORGA A51”, where BUSORGA and A51 are the subject code and section, respectively, to telephone number 2333. After a few seconds, the student will receive information on the subject in this format “BUSORGA A51 #41; MWF 1030-1130h, Rm M415. As of 1414h 10/05/2000.”

If the student only wishes to see the enrollment count of all the sections for a particular subject all he needs to do is to send the subject code in the text message in this format “DLSU SUBJ BUSORGA” and it will return “BUSORGA C31 #44; C32 #44; A51 #41; As of 1421h 10/05/2000.”

Due to the favorable responses that we got from the students and administrators of DLSU, it has been decided to offer more DLSU Infotext services such as important DLSU announcements (eg. No classes), DLSU contact information (tel, fax, email), e-mail activation, list of dial up lines for faculty and students and a lot more. Also, due to the request of some students, the ITC is also studying the possibility of making this service available via other GSM cellular phone companies (Smart, Islacom and Piltel).

ITC will be announcing other Infotext services as soon as they are available. Also, the ITC is accepting suggestions or ideas of other Infotext services useful to the DLSU community.
