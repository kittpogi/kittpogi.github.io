    Office of the Director Updates



  1. Updating documentation of OD-related procedures (ongoing)

  2. Participating in:
    1. CCS-Filinvest committee
      The opening of CCS-Filinvest is moved to June 2002.
    2. GSB-RCBC committee
      The planning for GSB-RCBC will continue with the recent decision of Administration to keep GSB as part of PSI and not to make it as DLSU Henry Sy School of Business (unofficial name).
    3. On-line learning committee
      Coordinating with vendors on on-line learning products and meeting with the committee on related matters like facilities, faculty training, and so on. 
    4. Committee on the renovation of M306 into a "smart" classroom 
    5. Project team for the construction of central administration building regarding network cabling ...turning over to cfo. 

  3. Initiating meeting with vendors on: 
    1. Microsoft windows media technologies 
    2. Microsoft videoconferencing technologies
    3. on-line learning technologies 
    4. upgrading satellite link for internet access 
    5. upgrading campus network infrastructure

  4. Identifying metrics on IT projects in campus 
    This is to focus on targets guiding the pace and priorities of regular activities and special projects.  This is in addition to current metrics like:
    1. improving computer-to-student- bandwidth-to-student ratios
    2. software installation time
    3. hardware repair time
    4. server uptime
    5. dial-up lines uptime
    6. network uptime
    7. internet uptime
    8. customer service satisfaction
    9. and so on

    The initial target is to network the entire campus on or before March 2001.  Another is to determine and improve the computer-to-faculty ratio per department.  Another metric is the training/education of university units on TQM (specialized by PRIO) and customer service (specialized by our help desk).  The initial focus is on service units -- library, registrar's office, accounting office, administrative services another set of targets is on information/processes like

    1. reports/information available
      - host-based or client/server-based
      - web-based (intranet/internet)
      - wireless
      - hardcopy-only
      ...versus information systems development/enhancement
    2. streamlining of University processes
    3. computer-based teaching materials
      - web-based
      - powerpoint
      ...versus hardcopy (transparency) and manual (board-based)

  5. Adhoc committees: 
    1. awards - reviewing incentives and related details 
    2. team-building - organizing team-building workshop 
    3. 5s - conducting spot audits 
  6. Pending project: 
    Review qs/jd and performance evaluation instruments to integrate more detailed items on
    1. quality performance
    2. continuous improvement
    3. other performance-based measurements
