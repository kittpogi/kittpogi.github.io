    ITP Incentive Update
 

Revisions to the Attendance Policy and Incentive Allocation Fund

Attendance in ITC plays a major role in the exercise of the daily functions of the staff.  Hence, an incentive was allocated for those who will maintain a perfect record of attendance for the term (Individual Term-based Perfect Attendance).  One term equals four  straight months including the trimestral and summer breaks (eg, June to September, and so on).

The criteria for this incentive is constantly subject to revision based on the ITC implementing guidelines.  The revisions made for the second term, effective October 2 are the following:

  1. For ITPs without leave entitlement (less than one year of service), 15 days leave allowance (leave without pay) are given for the school year.   Upon filing of the leave without pay, the ITP should classify the leave as Sick or Vacation leave - the 1.5 VL limit per month should be maintained in order to qualify for the perfect attendance.
  1. ITPs should have no multiple log-in/log-out for the day to qualify for the term-based perfect attendance.

It was also agreed that if the ITP assembly will not meet the average number of late for the term, at most 2.50 for this school year, the allotted incentive will be added to the individual term-based perfect attendance budget (still maintaining the condition that each of the awardees will only receive a maximum of P1,500).  

At the end of the school year, if there will be an excess in the allocated budget for the two attendance awards (Individual & ITP Assembly), the amount will be given to the staff with perfect attendance for the year.  If no one gets the perfect attendance award for the year, then the amount will be given to the Excellence awardee for the school year.  On the other hand, if there will be no excess amount, the ITC Director promised to find a budget for the Perfect Attendance awardee for the year.

Congratulations to the Individual Perfect Attendance Awardees for the First Term (June to September)!!!
