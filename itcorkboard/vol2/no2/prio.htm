    PRIO Updates
 

Busy Buddies Corner

After the storm of Fernando Carillo (FC) ever wondered how PRIO faired with him? If FC made every one go “agaga” during his recent concert stint in 
Manila … PRIO made the PRIT members of Adjustment, as well as every students who are adjusting, a bit more comfortable during the last concluded two-day Adjustment period. I guess with the tandem of our own version of Fernando Mamarillo a.k.a. JGK and Thalia y Rosalinda a.k.a PBA, we could safely say that we also give entertainment to where it is much needed by the people we want to serve. Of course this would have not been possible without the help and support of their manager Boss del Rosario a.k.a ADS.

Aside from the concert of FC … he also did some interviews and skydiving drills. How about our Fernando Mamarillo? Well, he made his debut project with WIMO. Now he is still getting some much-needed data to plot out the project. Part of the support extended to him by the Boss was giving WIMO their Basic TQM training to level with the goal of the University.  Recently, he just had his weeklong trainings. Did he meet the girl of his dream there?… Better check the glow in his eyes!

How about our mama mia Rosalinda Trisha, she also went with Fernando Mamarillo on the weeklong training, and still busy with her Adjustment write-up. Wait for the new updates… she is very persistent in targeting to finish the project this term. Why is she that persistent? Well, the Boss gave a challenging task to PRIO that is now called the PSI project with the goal of having PSI set up its own system. Imagine this task to be targeted this December!?! Well, it may be too big a project to finish but somehow PRIO will manage this smoothly… right guys!?!

If FC sung to everybody’s delight, so did PRIO in coordination with OD with the much-talked TGIF activities last month. Our master showman GERMS (btw, this is the other name of JGK) will give you another spectacular presentation come the month of the hearts. So expect more from the leading authority of gimmicks!

That’s all for now… life is kool and I love it!

 

Eileen D. Manabat 10/12/00
