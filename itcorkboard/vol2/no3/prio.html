ANOTHER LIFE AFTER A YEAR
By Eileen Manabat

Life really gets tough each day don't you think so?
Then try assessing life after a year. Have you accomplished those long lists of things to do or things to change, in other words those resolutions you have promised yourself? It was once said that a measure of a man is when he lives up to his words. Well resolutions are words listed or said? Can you say that you are now a man of your own words? What am I arriving at? Duh, I don't know. but this seems to be a nice start for the article so hear me out.

Another year has passed and the laughable PRIO has survived the gusty winds and strong typhoons of the Dragon year 2000. Listed here are its triumph over the turbulence of life:

  1. JGK's WIMO Project was put on hold to give priority to the HUGE PSI project. Currently, his project is on Accounting processes and is on stage two of its draft. As I have mentioned to the previous article, there were those nice glow in his eyes for this project. Hmmm, better ask this guy for the details of his secret project other than the Accounting System and Procedures, right Joel!?! Are you all wondering what it is all about? The clue may be short , ask the guy is he has found his match!
  2. PBA's assigned PSI project is all about the Registrar's Office Systems and Procedures. The second draft is ongoing as planned. On the other hand, the Adjustment Project is still undergoing minor revisions before it will be up for turn over to the Process Owner for approval of the EVP.
  3. We attended some training/seminar to orient us on give us the latest trends in our field. Everyone is encouraged to conduct echo training. PRIO and OD joined forces in belting out a jingle on their own interpretation of the Good Housekeeping Five S.

As I end our list of conquests, may I add that now PRIO has two gentlemen to serve you. The first one was introduced a year ago by the name of Fernando Mamarillo(remember this infamous guy?) , now let us get to know our "rooster" for the year of the Snake to accompany PRIO in soaring high to reach its goals. curtain calls to. "Mr. Jojo Velarde" or legally (any motion to disprove this ?), he is Mr. Teodoro M. Soliva Jr.! Does his first name sound so serious? Maybe that's also the reason why he insists to be called plain and simple and yet the jolly "Jojo:.

My First Week's Experiences at ITC
by: Jojo M. Soliva Jr.

I started here at De La Salle University - Professional Schools Inc. exactly on the 2nd day of the first year of the millennium. I arrived ten minutes before seven in the morning trying to catch-up the eight o'clock start of my work miscalculating the traffic condition along Taft Avenue.

The Lobby guard kept me waiting until nine o'clock since Amalia D. Salcedo and Rosalia N. Nicolas who happened to be my contact persons were not around that day.

Patricia B. Antonio and Eileen D. Manabat were so accommodating to the extent of asking me if I need some more things to know regarding the working environment and the ITC rules and regulations.

Joel G. Kisteria did an extra effort of touring me around the campus. I spent the rest of the day browsing the web site of DLSU to know more about La Sallian values and standard operation procedures at the Information Technology Center.
