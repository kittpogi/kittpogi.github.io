Housekeeping Committee (5S) : Etcetera

PDCA of 5S
by Rina U. Alano

PLAN
- Organize a plan committee
- Develop a plan for the first 3-S's (Seiri-Sort, Seiton-Systematize, Seiso-Sweep)
- Formally announce and promote the 5-S program
- Train and educate

DO
- Everyone practices housekeeping and discipline
- Everyone practices workplace organization

CHECK
- Evaluate and recognize

ACT
- Correct Actions
- Standardize

PDCA of 5S is a cycle, so make it a part of our daily activities.


Start 5S at your own desk first!
by Rina U. Alano

Pencils, pens, erasers, notebooks and staplers are common things that we use daily at our work. Where can you find these stuffs? Where else but in our office desks or tables. A well-arranged table drawer at our workplace ensures visibility and simpler monitoring of our things.

Here are some advantages of having an organized office desk:

  1. Each item is traced easily.
  2. Unwanted items are removed through Seiri (sort).
  3. Missing items are detected easily.
  4. Searching time is eliminated, thus net working time is increased.
  5. Frustration and distress in searching are prevented.

So, let it be our habit to sort, clean, and organize our office desk daily.
