by Flor Tamonan

Let our awardees and all those who excel in their field be our model, an inspiration, ….. a shining example. Let not the fire in our heart to achieve the peak of success fade, be bold and continuously strive to be better, for the love of work and for the passion for excellence.

One of a great people of our time once said:

" In everything I do, I do my best, and if it turns out right, nothing can be said against me but if it turns out wrong, even if ten angels say it's right, it would not make any difference."

May we always make a better difference in everything we do and may we always let our light shine!

Congratulations to all.
