DLSU emerges champion in the UAAP
by: Francis Mark Santos
The Green Archers celebrate their 4th title.

Victory can never be sweeter than this! The DLSU green archers overcame all obstacles hurled at their path to achieve their fourth straight UAAP title, and the whole lasallian community were behind them all the way. From the students up to the administrators, wearing green or white, they all trekked to the big dome on the big day to show their support for the team. Of course, only those with their tickets made it.

Before the big day, it was funny to observe students very willing to fall in a long line at the accounting office (from as far as the south gate)and even missing their classes to buy UAAP tickets but very impatient when enrolling or paying for their tuition fees.The more impatient ones even bought their tickets from very enterprising scalpers posted in different areas of Taft avenue (heard some reports that scalpers abound in Katipunan avenue, too!). Buying tickets from scalpers means you would be paying, if you were lucky, 100% more than the original price of the ticket. And as the big day drew near, the cost of the ticket would jack up to as much as five to ten times the original value.

There were even rumors that the Alumni Office pegged the price of a P100.00 ticket to double and treated the difference as donation. Rumors have it that alumni wishing to purchase tickets from the Office have to update their association membership dues just to be entitled to the prized tickets.

I do not wish to portray an holier-than-thou persona here, or dampen the spirit of the celebration. These were all observations anyway.

Animo La Salle!
