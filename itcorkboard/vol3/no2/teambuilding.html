Teambuilding Committee Updates : Updates
 by: Joel Kristeria

We're already on our 2nd term of the school year 2001-2002. That means, it's "d'Ber" months now. Our calendar says there are 4 "BER" months, with the last month being the one we await with enthusiasm….(for most, I guess)

Anyway, the ITC's TBC is already planning some events to be enjoyed by everyone. But before that, here are some of the activities that took place last 1st term….

CAN U ? CAN CRUSHING · The can (crushing) collection is still going on. Ms Celine Rhaniel & Lorna are exchanging the crushed cans at selected exchange booths. The generated fund from this activity is intended for the ITC's Outreach events. For the outreach, of course, the TBC is still pursuing some schedules or perhaps other alternatives to extend the fund to the chosen foundation or organization. Right now, another set of boxes and plastics has been put in place mostly here in the Gokongwei building. AND TO THOSE STRESSED AND WILLING ITPs, WE'LL BE NEEDING YOU FROM TIME TO TIME TO POUR YOUR STRESS INTO THESE CANS..crush natin sila ha!

HEALTH BUFFS · Guess what guys???? ITPs are sure becoming health buffs now-a-days. Basketball games are still being scheduled by the growing number of GUYS in ITC. And in addition, (In preparation to the sportsfest) aficionados of ping-pong & badminton are going to the 8th floor of the Staples Center..oops, the DLSU Sports building pala to practice and smash force to those ping-pong balls and shuttlecocks (ouch!! That mustdAh hurt dude!) . And for those of you who are really interested in sweating all those pounds off……. there's a weight training gym on the 9th floor, let's pump some iron and trim those bodies, ok!

STRESSED OUT ? · We're hoping that Ms Daisy's "Stress Management" training will push through this term, so that we can all have that really relaxing and learning experience, coping and dealing with stress in work and in everyday life.

TGIHTGIF-It seems that "F" is slowly retiring. "tH" is making it's climb to bump off "F" as the last item of this "acronym". As started by the DAO-Docu group, the usual activity was done on a fabulous THURSDAY, twice on the month of September……and we sure had some participation on those Thursdays J. It was also a great way of hitting 2 birds with 1 stone, coz we didn't just had a TGIH…we also crushed those cans!!

HAPPY B-DAY TO U · For the Birthday events… although there were some flaws on some BirthMonths…the TBC would like to apologize for that. Rest assured that your BirthdayEventOrganizers are doing their jobs to make it a simple yet encouraging day once your birthday comes. AND SA LAHAT NG NAGPATIKIM NG KANILANG MASAYANG KAARAWAN …. NAWA'Y….UHMMM, MAULIT NEXT YEAR ..heheeh… so Happy na Birthday pa, ok

And as we look forward to the last "BER" month of this year, the "new faces" ( madami po sila) of ITC is already looking for venues and coordinating plans to make this much anticipated event another success for us ITPs…plus of course they have to consider the ITPs growing appetite for the best deli in the land ( teka baka sumobra layo, o sige, sa nearby area na lang….) advance happy Christmas to y'all!!
