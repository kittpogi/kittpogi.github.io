Computer Facilities Office : Updates


New Acquisitions
by: Nicasio Bonao


ITC acquired Compaq Proliant M750 server for the faculty mail server and two Compaq Proliant ML570 for Portal and Web application servers. It is expected that these upgrades will improve our server's performance in terms of availability, reliability, and speed.




Network Expansions
by: Nicasio Bonao

Network expansion is ongoing for the Velasco and St. Joseph Buildings. Wireless LAN is likewise installed at the University Library, Gokongwei Ground Floor, North Conservatory, and GSB-RCBC Plaza. This facility would provide students more avenues in connecting to the Internet.

Hardware and Software Acquisition Plans
by: Nicasio Bonao

To secure the DLSU networks, CFO is currently evaluating several Firewall and Anti-Virus softwares. On the other hand, CFO also plans to acquire Storage Area Networks (SAN) to externalize and consolidate enterprise storage and help create continuous operations and disaster tolerance.

Manual faxing is out of date, and on top of that, it is expensive. The solution? CFO is eyeing the use of Fax server, a native connector for Microsoft Exchange. This seamless integration allows users to send and receive faxes directly from Microsoft Outlook, making it easy to use and learn. Using Fax server can save the organization substantial costs, and can improve customer service and image.
