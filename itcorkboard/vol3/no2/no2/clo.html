Computer Laboratory Operations : Updates

M306: Smart Classroom
 by: Glen Bertulfo

Ever seen a real smart classroom? How about visiting Miguel 306 and have a feel of what a Smart Classroom truly is.

The former auditorium classroom had been renovated early this year to accommodate a University-first, state-of-the-art, Smart Classroom. This was spearheaded by the Library Director and conceptualized by the excellent brains of people from the IT Center and CREM under the tutelage of Bro.Mike Rapatan.

The room is equipped with a surround sound system, a pair of hi-tech multimedia projectors and of course the essential component, the Robotel Smart Class System that enable students and faculty members to collaborate and even discuss classsroom work on a real time, per PC per student basis. It also boasts of a multi-directional pan/zoom/tilt camera which prepares the room for a possible Video Conferencing session in the near future.

In case, you have not seen it, feel free to drop by and be treated to an event you would never forget in this lifetime.
