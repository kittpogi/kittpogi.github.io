Teambuilding Committee : Etcetera

Goodbye 2001…Hello 2002
by Joel G. Kisteria

"Another year over…a new one just begun" - John Lennon

Just like the song, we have just accomplished another year. And before we go on with our line-up of activities for this New Year, let's take the time to "… FLASHBACK last quarter 2001"…

…the TGIF-TGIH, movies, videoke and the invasion of the game/quiz shows became a regular activity. SMO did well with their "Wheel Of Fortune" game and DAO-Docu's Videoke/Name That Tune Challenge…and the nice part of it, there are food!!!

It's a good thing that the TBC "can collection project" was somehow aligned to the TGIF-TGIH activity. We get to hit two birds with one stone. The cans were crushed during the activity.

…the choir members are continuously singing their hearts out to the Lord. Thanks to the leadership of Miss Vivs who had another "gift" delivered just recently. Rhaniel, Lorna, Leah, Flor, Ampel, Jojo, Rina, Janet, Jean, keep it up!

… the Outreach activity finally pushed through. Celine, Agnes, Daisy and Joel went to the "Home for the Dying Destitutes" last December to endorse the money that the ITC TBC had gathered from the "can collection project". The money however didn't just come from the cans… the generous people of ITC still contributed funds for this particular event, majority from the December birthday celebrants. Half of the total amount collected was used to purchase short pants for the residents of the "home" while the other half was endorsed as cash. Though they still wanted volunteer service in addition to the donation, we believe that we have done a good deed. Hopefully, some of us, if not all, can go and render services to them. Just let us know and we'll tell you how…

…finally, the culminating Christmas Party at Kamayan Restaurant (part dieux, we were there last year J ). The newbies made sure that we had a different ITC Christmas experience though we're in the same venue. And the Disney characters came to life in this year's presentations. The different groups formed by the committee rendered their interpretation of this year's theme "Disney Christmas at ITC"…It was bested by "Peter Pan (fafa)" group…Dance, games, prizes and of course…FOOOOOOD made this year's celebration really worth reminiscing, plus the fact that we saw the "balbonic male-belly dancers" from the newbies. There was also the traditional "monito-monita", which means…gifts, gifts, and more gifts. Kay daming pictures..pero parang ala pa akong nakikita ah?? L

LET'S ALL LOOK FORWARD FOR 2002's SUPRISES…peace y'all.
