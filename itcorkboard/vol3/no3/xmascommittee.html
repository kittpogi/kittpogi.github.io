Disney at ITC: The Christmas Party Celebration
By Flor Tamonan

A parade of abounding talents, wit and creativity, that's what was seen during the ITC's preparation for the Holiday Season's celebration.


Click here for Picture Gallery.

The Christmas Party Committee, having primed the activities for the occasion, made a tremendous effort to create a celebration a bit different than usual. Each department was required to decorate their rooms according to their notion of a particular Christmas Song. The committee called for a group presentation imitating the cartoon characters of Disney, gift exchanges, a simple but entertaining program, and of course a special dance and song number from committee member imitating Aladdin, Princess Jasmine and the Arabian Night dancers.

During the celebration, there were those who made an extra effort to make their presentations apparently better than others, and it bore fruit. Below is a Christmas Party re-cap of winners:

Office Decoration Award:
DOCU/DAO Office for conceptualizing Winter Wonderland, bringing to life the atmosphere of winter Christmas.

Group Christmas Presentation:
Group II - (Vivian Sy, Loveleah Mallari, Patricia Antonio, Sally Nicolas, Johnny Benitez, Brian Co, Glen Bertulfo, Jhun Galang, Leo Magsisi. These are the people who re-enacted the children's all-time-favorite Peter Pan Christmas adventure in Neverland.

Lucky raffle draw winners:
1st Prize - Lorna Acorda
2nd Prize - Vivian Sy
3rd Prize - Brian Co

We thank everyone (including our guests and judges) who made that simple celebration worth reminiscing and reminded us of the true meaning of Christmas, not only on that day but every minute, every hour, everyday.
