PRIO TRAINING VENTURES
By Joel Kisteria

PRIO…and we're into training too…

Late last year, PRIO was busy in coming up with training modules aimed to institute quality concepts and issues to the center and to the University.

The TQM training module objectives are:

  • To create awareness and appreciation of the TQM Concepts, principles, tools and approaches, and their application in the Academic institutions.
  • To enhance the capability of faculty members and school administrators and staff in integrating TQM in appropriate school courses/activities.

Amy is concentrating on the Quality Management System, from which the quality standards of the University may be developed. And hopefully, this would bear fruit and that the University would pursue the fundamental concepts underlying this subject. This for sure will boost the claim of the University of quality education.

Jojo and I were assigned to come up with the TQM Training module (that includes the PRIT approach). Recall that PRIT has always been synonymous with PRIO, and now the concept of "Quality Circle" is absorbed through the PRIT. The module includes the instruction of practical TQM tools that can be easily used and implemented.

These are just the initial modules. PRIO will soon be coming up with other modules aligned to increase quality awareness in all units in the University.

So…you will be seeing more of us preaching the quality principles.

We hope to get your support on this.

Busy Buddies
By Rina U. Alano

All Christmas presents have been opened…another Christmas has gone by…the challenge of facing another year in our lives is at hand…

But before we start the 3rd Term for SY 2001-2002, let us look back and recall what made us busy this past term…

Busy Buddy Rina

RINA has prepared a matrix to define features and requirements of information needed for the GSB Updating of Student Information. Up to this date, she is still waiting for feedback from GSB.

RINA revised several parts of the PRIO Manual for its improvement and also revised and finalized the 5S Training Module.

RINA's project on the GSB Enrollment Process has been shelved because the recommendations made as a result of the Time Study conducted and observations made, could not be implemented. GSB has already decided to implement a different procedure for this 3rd Term.

Aside from handling her own projects, RINA is busy managing the group with their assigned projects.

Busy Buddy Amy

AMY, together with JANET, has completed the documentation and proposal of the Manila Green and White Yearbook Subscription Procedure. This would be presented to the Coordinator of Green and White for feedback.

AMY has also completed the Quality Management System Module. This has already been reviewed by SPM. At present, the module is in its finalization stage.

AMY has simulated and documented the flow of application and renewal of scholarship grant of STUFAP in order to evaluate the location of the process. She would turn over this project to JOJO this term.

Busy Buddy Joel

JOEL, together with JOJO, has completed the TQM Training Module. This has already been reviewed by SPM. At present, the module is in its finalization stage.

JOEL has resumed the documentation of the PSI Accounting procedures since the project has been discontinued towards the end of SY2000-2001.

Busy Buddy Jojo

Aside from the completed TQM Training Module, JOJO has assisted RINA in the revision of PRIO Manual.

JOJO has designed and completed the template for the Quality System Document (Quality Manual) by considering the comments of the other ITC offices regarding the template.

Busy Buddy Janet

In addition to the documentation and proposal of the Manila Green and White Yearbook Subscription Procedure, JANET has interviewed the HR staff (Manila) for the PSI-HR Process Design. Presently, she is documenting the ITC Recruitment and Hiring process.

JANET has completed the Customer Service Module. The module shall be used for the trainings to be conducted by PRIO this 3rd Term.

Busy Buddy Tricia

TRICIA has completed the Registrar's Office Manual. It is in its finalization stage. This would be submitted to the Registrar's Office for their approval.

TRICIA has designed and documented the Documentation Standards that can be used by ITC as a guide in their documentation. SPM and the Documentation Office have already reviewed this. It is in its finalization stage.


Whew! That's about it…

Looking ahead, expect Quality Awareness from us…such as TQM trainings, Quality Tips, Quality Updates, etc… Let me end this article by saying…it has been a good term indeed!
