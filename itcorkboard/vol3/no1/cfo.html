Computer Facilities Office : Updates

CFO Projects
by: Nicasio Bonao

The Computer Facilities Office are presently in the thick of the battle, so to speak, with these current projects:

  • Acquisition of additional 72 new dial-up lines for students and faculty.
  • Acquisition of additional 1 unit of Remote Access Server (RAS) to support the new dial-up lines.
  • Acquisition of Compaq Proliant 8000 server for Faculty Mail server.
  • Network expansion plans for CCS and the North Wing of the Conservatory have been completed. While St. John Hall, Velasco and SJ Buildings are ongoing.
  • Plan to acquire Storage Area Networks. The purpose of SAN is to externalize and consolidate enterprise storage and help create continuous operations and disaster tolerance.
