Querying Masterfiles Using Oracle Application Server
by: Julio Ceres Ibay

The Data Administration Office is working on having an online procedure for querying the University's Masterfile. Initially, searching for records in the Course Masterfile and School Masterfile will be made available through the Oracle Application Server. The search program will be installed in the server and our users can run the program using any standard Internet browser from their respective offices (where Internet connections are available). We hope to eliminate costly and time consuming process of providing hard copies of such files by providing a more up-to-date, easily accessible, and less costly means of providing information to our users for reference purposes.

My First Month in La Salle
by: Florinda Tamonan

The first day, I was skeptic whether I will love working in LaSalle? Will the people welcome me? As I read on references about ITC-DLSU and as I get to know the people that I will be working with, I began to see things differently.

The second, third and fourth day, I began to appreciate every simple joys presented to me by these people, assisting me in their own simple ways to adapt to this new environment, giving me new learning horizon and patiently teaching me get along. Acquaintance grew further when they invited me to be a member of the ITC choir! Imagine, without them hearing my voice, they welcomed me. Big mistake! But kidding aside, it's a good feeling singing for Him.

I believed in the mission and vision of DLSU, and as Bro.Roly and Mr. Gonzales delivered their first inspiring message for this school year, it further intensified. The institution's passion to uplift the standard of living by providing educational excellence, outreach program and the like were inevitable, and being a part of it is a great privilege.

I was lucky I was able to witness the ITC day celebration. I saw how everyone contributed to the celebration. The works were remarkable. I envisioned the limitless efforts of everyone to give better service to the habitue of ITC and of the DLSU as a whole.

A bit nervous, well I guessed that was what I felt during the first Staff Meeting for the school year, not because of anything, but because I'll be singing! Listeners were very silent, maybe Janet and I sang like Ibong Adarnas, they were enchanted by our voice. I found this meeting very advantageous, the knowledge-sharing, the accomplishments, the activities and the great achievement of every individual. Very inspiring indeed! The ITC guessing game was fun, I could hardly believe how cute these people were during their childhood or should I say during their toddler years. Unfortunately, I was not able to give a single correct answer. Looks like I'm really not good on individual facial profiling.

The travel distance home to La Salle that I thought would be a great sacrifice was not bad after all. The people are amicable, and it's like an extension of my previous confidant. In my work, I'm beginning to harbor on visions on this new arena that I am exploring. And now, I just feel that everything was moving in perfect synchronicity.

Let me end this story of mine by extending my gratitude to everyone. I used the word "gratitude" meaning thank you and hoping for your future support.

"I WILL CONTINUE, O MY GOD, TO DO ALL MY ACTIONS FOR THE LOVE OF YOU!"
