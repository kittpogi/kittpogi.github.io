Silver Girl

Rosalia R. Nicolas
Secretary, Information Technology Center

A 91.285% performance rating average for loyalty, dedication and merit covering the period 1995-2002 (a total of 7 years)… Perfect Attendance Awards from 1989 to 1999 (that’s 10 consecutive years)…

This is what Ms. Sally has to show for her silver anniversary in La Salle!

Ms. Sally is the one person who can boast that she has been with the Information Technology Center (then known as La Salle Computer Center), since its early stages.

She has risen from the ranks, so to speak, from being a clerk typist, a laboratory technician, and the ITC Secretary to top it all off.

One thing I bet you didn’t know. She graduated with the degree Bachelor of Science in Commerce major in Marketing at the Far Eastern University.

Another thing, in the school year 2002-2003, again, she was a Perfect Attendance Awardee. And she has no plans of stopping there. (You want proof? On the morning of May 27, 2003, she braved the high waters of Malabon albeit the strong winds and pouring rains to come to the office before 8:00 a.m. only to find out that classes and offices were suspended.) The start of another decade of coming to the office promptly? You bet!
