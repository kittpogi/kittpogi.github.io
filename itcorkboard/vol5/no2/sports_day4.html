Conquering Mt. Pulag: A Hesitant Climber’s Account: Day 4

Day 4, Aug. 24, 8:00 am

When I woke up, Pres and her friends have already gone back to Manila. Lorna, Jojo, Ryan, Joel, and Francis were all packing their things for their 10:20 am bus ride back to Manila while Harold, Tetet, and I were making plans for our extended vacation in Baguio.


Post Script, Sep 26, 10:00 pm
Some of you may be wondering. Was it worth it? Do I regret having joined the group? Was climbing Mt. Pulag one of the hardest experiences of my life? Does Harold really not take a bath every day?

Allow me to answer this way. I have decided that mountain climbing is not for me and that as much as I can help it, I will only climb one mountain in my entire lifetime and that mountain is Mt. Pulag. I have also decided that no matter what happens, I will one day see the sun rise from Mt. Pulag’s peak.
