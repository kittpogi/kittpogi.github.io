World’s Top 5 Supercomputers

According to the www.top500.org, of the 500 systems, 51% of the top 500 systems which includes seven of the top 10 systems are installed in the United States. Of the same top 500 systems, 92% are produced in the United States (US). These figures reflect the leadership of US in the use and production of high-performance computing. These supercomputers are ranked according to processing speed rated on benchmark performance in “teraflops” or trillions of calculations per second (Tflops).
