ITC Annual Summer Outing 2003 : Wonder Island Calamba, Laguna

Our Wonder Island Summer Outing
by: Precious Anne L. Taliwaga

As early as the February ITC Staff Meeting, all of us were already looking forward to the Annual Summer Outing, though it’s still a good three months away. Who wouldn’t be? What with the barely bearable heat that we all endured since early March! Another thing, it was held just at the outskirts of Manila. Due to its proximity to our home bases, just a handful were not able to make it.

see the rest of the outing pictures in the image gallery

Road Trip

Assembly time was before daybreak, 5:30 a.m. to be exact, for us who chose DLSU as our jump-off point. With all the warnings that we will leave not a minute after our agreed-upon time, we still waited for all who didn’t hear their alarm clocks ring and those who were still in dreamland by departure time.

Next stopover… Alabang. Since a number of us reside in that area and it was along the way to our final destination, we let them enjoy the few more minutes to stay in bed and pick them up at 6:30.

Highway to Wonder Island

The Outing Committee composed of the newbies on the block, wanted everyone to enjoy every minute of the outing, even the bus trip. The people who counted on sleeping during the ride couldn’t help but keep their eyes open as trivia questions were thrown one after the other. It went on for quite awhile. Well, they were able to shut their eyes too. When they awoke, it was time for the boat ride.

Arrival… Games… Awards

First order of business…fun games. Laughter filled the air as the few opted to just grin from ear-to-ear while players did their best to win in…

Animal Sounds… Great minds think alike… Seldom does a team member fail to call out the right team. As the game went on, the faster the exchanges in calling out other teams, the more synchronized the team players were.

The Boat is Sinking… Need I remind you how this game ended? Has fate thrown these two people to bag the championship? Did our very own “Rose and Jack” really sink as the Titanic chose the winners? Your guess is as good as mine.

Softdrink Drinking Contest… You thought this was THE game everyone can join, right? We were all proven wrong? Contestants, how does it feel to drink in Coke and air at the same time?

Longest Line… How did the winning team make their line so long? Where did all those things come from?

Carry-Me Race… This just proves that men and women ITPs alike are fit. The men smilingly, and effortlessly – I should add – transported our ladies from one point and back.

Food

What would an outing be without food? Where will we get the energy to join in all these activities?

It was a good thing the food supply was abundant as all savored and enjoyed breakfast, lunch and merienda. There were a few leftovers because some of us suddenly became conscious of how much we have already eaten and suddenly remembered to resume our diets.

Still all the laughing, shouting, running around and swimming used up so much of our energy and made us hungry every so often so we just ATE ALL WE CAN anyway.

Raffle

Some people have all the luck. 8 ITPs were able to bring home a camera each and 4 more toted home jackets.

Swimming

After a heavy lunch, everybody ran towards the pool to do what we came there for, in the first place… SWIMMING.
