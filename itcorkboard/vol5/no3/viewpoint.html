Faith: Our People’s Greatest Treasure

by: Irwin Jurilla

In the early days, man found ways to record his work. Though it took him a number of centuries to do this, he was able to improve on things from the time they were invented/discovered. In the year 3500 BC, expressions were made through drawings in clay tablets in Sumer - a country in southeastern Mesopotamia - the birthplace of the first civilization. By the year 1500 BC, the Phoenician alphabet was used. To date, one of the greatest inventions is paper by T’sai Lun in 105 BC. Without this invention, it could have taken man longer to be more progressive.

In Philippine history, numerous heroes have wagered their lives over the centuries. As a nation, our growth in civilization never came so fast or as readily before us - hundreds of years with heroes giving their lives and a people power struggle to gain the long lost democracy.

Though democracy has been gained, the real spirit of it is yet to be experienced by our people. Government services rarely reach far-flung areas. Corruption in the government has been steadily in the headlines of daily journals. The nation has been in debt with billions of dollars.

Yet our faith and determination as a nation had always been strong and firm. No tragedies have ever been damaging, no dictators have ever been too strong to hold back the will of freedom-loving Filipinos. In times of confusion and disorder, it is in the heart of every Filipino to rise up and show the way to greatness and magnanimity.

People power is the legacy we leave to the rest of the nations who had been struggling from bondage to be a free country. Never in modern time’s history of nations has people power been so successful in dethroning a dictator.

Long inhabited by the wandering people of Asia, these group of islands stood to be a nation of people proud of its faith. Our faith has always been our greatest treasure. It has been our rallying point in all times. It unites us all into one nation, the Philippines. Our faith is the backbone of our nation.
