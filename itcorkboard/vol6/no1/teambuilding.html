New Teambuilding Format
Contributed by: Harold Paulo D. Bautista

Starting July 2004, the teambuilding committee will be implementing a new format for the teambuilding sessions, a.k.a. TGIH/TGIF. Under the new format, the different offices are grouped together as follows:

Group 1: SMO Support Team, DAO, DOCU
Group 2: PRIO, OD, SysAds
Group 3: Helpdesk, PCNet
Group 4: SMO Student Team, SMO Employee Team

Every month, a group will organize the TGIH/TGIF activity based on a theme chosen by the teambuilding committee. The organizing group shall implement the activity together with another group. The schedule of group interactions is as follows:
